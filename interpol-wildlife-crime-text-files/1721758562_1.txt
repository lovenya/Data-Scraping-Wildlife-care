St Johann im Pongau, AustriaThe European Regional Conference takes place annually in order to assess the state of police cooperation and the fight against transnational crime. It brings together Heads of Police and police cooperation bodies in the region to discuss specific crime-related problems.Key issued to be considered during the gathering include:border securityterrorismcybercrimeorganized crimecooperation between INTERPOL and national law enforcement in Europe
