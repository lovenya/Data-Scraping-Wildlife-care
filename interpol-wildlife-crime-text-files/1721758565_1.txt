18 – 20 January 2017 – Kathmandu, NepalThe Asian Regional Conference takes place every two years in order to assess the state of police cooperation and the fight against transnational crime. It brings together Heads of Police and police cooperation bodies in the region to discuss specific crime-related problems.Key issued to be considered during the Asian gathering include:TerrorismEnvironmental securityHuman TraffickingBorder securityCybercrime
