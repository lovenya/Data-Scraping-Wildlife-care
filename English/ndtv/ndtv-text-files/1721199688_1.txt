They forcibly put up banners carrying pictures of PM Modi... The banners belonging to the AAP government were torn off," Mr Rai alleged at a press conference.The Delhi Police warned people not to touch banners carrying pictures of PM Modi, he claimed.Lieutenant Governor VK Saxena and Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal were supposed to attend the event and all preparations had been made, he said."An event of the Kejriwal government has been turned into a political event of PM Modi.
