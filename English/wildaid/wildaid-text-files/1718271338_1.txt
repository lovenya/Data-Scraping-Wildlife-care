SB 680 is not only good for the future of sharks, it’s good for divers, fishers, the environment and our economy. It’s precisely why this environmentalist stands solidly in support and why I urge Gov. Ron DeSantis to sign it into law, state Rep. Kristin Jacobs writes in an op-ed State Rep. Kristin Jacobs, a Democrat, represents the 96th House District of Florida, which includes Coconut Creek, Margate, Coral Springs and Parkland in Broward County.
