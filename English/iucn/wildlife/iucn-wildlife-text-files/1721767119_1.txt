We invite proposals for contributions to the Conference from all sectors and disciplines to share and discuss experiences, insights, research findings and ideas.We particularly encourage proposals that create constructive interactions among people from different viewpoints, across sectors and disciplines.

Please read the Aims of the Conference before preparing your abstract, as these will guide the abstract selection decision process.

Please feel free to share information about the conference with your networks, colleagues and friends. The conference is organised in collaboration with the IUCN Commission on Environmental, Economic and Social Policy (CEESP), the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) of the United Nations, and the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) and several other organisations.
