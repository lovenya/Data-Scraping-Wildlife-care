The call is open for all Vietnamese citizens from 21 – 50 years old and interested in wildlife conservation and environmental protection. There are four options for volunteers to choose and each trip will last for 7 days with 14 volunteers/trip: (i) 06-12 July, (ii) 3- 9 August, (iii) 24- 30 August, (iv) 14 – 20 September. The number of volunteers each trip has been increased to meet the increasing demand of applicants.

Please see the programme details (in Vietnamese) for further information about requirements and responsibilities.
