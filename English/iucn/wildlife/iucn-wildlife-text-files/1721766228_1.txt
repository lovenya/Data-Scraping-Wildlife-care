The Species Day will bring together Solomon Islanders working on species conservation to celebrate the species of Solomon Islands, and promote species protection, management, recovery and rehabilitation. The focus will predominantly be on the priority species listed in CEPF’s investment strategy for Solomon Islands. 

A Call for Submissions for presentations and working group discussions is now open. Details are available here.

A photo competition aimed at CEPF grantees and others working in Solomon Islands is open concurrently. Winning images shall be showcased on the day of the Species Forum. Further details are available here.

The deadline for submission of abstracts and photographs is 31st August 2017.
