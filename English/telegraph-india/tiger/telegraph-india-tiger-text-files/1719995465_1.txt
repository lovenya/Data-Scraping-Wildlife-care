Allu Arjun-starrer Pushpa: The Rule tops a recent Ormax Media list of the five most-awaited Hindi movies in the last quarter of 2023, followed by Hera Pheri 3, Salaar, Tiger 3 and Bhool Bhulaiya 3. Unveiled on July 18, the list only considers films releasing after September 2022 and whose trailers are yet to be released. #OrmaxCinematix Most-awaited Hindi films, as on Jul 15, 2023 (only films releasing Sep 2023 onwards whose trailer has not released yet have been considered) pic.twitter.com/jzm6R1eQ1X The buzz around the Allu Arjun-starrer Pushpa: The Rule has been peaking ever since the ‘Where is Pushpa’ promotional campaign was launched on the actor’s birthday in April this year. The teaser of the Sukumar-helmed actioner showed how rumours of Pushpa’s death sparked riots in Tirupati. However, the ganglord was spotted in a forest scaring off a tiger. Pushpa’s androgynous avatar in the poster of the upcoming sequel of Pushpa: The Rise also left fans awestruck. Fans are also eagerly waiting for the reunion of Akshay Kumar, Paresh Rawal and Suniel Shetty in the much-awaited third instalment in the Hera Pheri franchise. Kisi Ka Bhai Kisi Ki Jaan director Farhad Samji is slated to helm this comedy. The teaser of Prabhas-starrer Salaar has also created a buzz among viewers. Directed by Prashanth Neel of KGF fame, The teaser of Salaar Part 1 - Ceasefire promises an action adventure that also features Prithviraj Sukumaran and Shruti Haasan. Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif’s spy action thriller Tiger 3 is slated for a Diwali release this year. Shah Rukh Khan is likely to make a cameo appearance as Pathaan in the movie. Kartik Aaryan is all set to reprise his role as Rooh Baba in Bhool Bhulaiyaa 3, scheduled to hit cinemas in Diwali, 2024. The actor recently took to social media to make the announcement alongside a short video featuring one of his popular dialogues from the 2022 blockbuster Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2.
