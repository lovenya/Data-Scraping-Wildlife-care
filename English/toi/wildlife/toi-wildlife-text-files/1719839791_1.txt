Panaji: In a case that indicates how terrestrial wildlife is also being stranded on state shores, a porcupine was found dead on Colomb beach in Canacona on Monday.
No details were available about the cause of mortality of this rodent with sharp quills that lives in forests, hills and green areas and eats grass, roots and other green food.

The marine strandings during the last two days follow a spate of cases of mortalities of porpoises and turtles on April 19. Five marine turtles and four finless porpoises had washed ashore dead at various beaches. Varca beach had the highest number of dead marine animals, including three porpoises and two turtles.
While cases of distress due to dehydration caused by heat and other reasons are being reported in the state, an injured tern (water bird) was also found in Calangute on Saturday.
