BENGALURU: Bengaluru Customs officials apprehended a passenger at Kempegowda International Airport on Monday for allegedly attempting to smuggle yellow anacondas hidden in his check-in luggage. The officials discovered 10 yellow anacondas concealed in the passenger's check-in bag upon his arrival from Bangkok.
