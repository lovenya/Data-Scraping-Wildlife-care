WAYANAD: A tiger was discovered trapped in a well in a residential area near Kalpetta in Wayanad, Kerala, on Wednesday. The family living in the vicinity found the tiger in the well while investigating water supply issues. 
'They alerted us, and a team of officials promptly responded,' stated a senior forest official.
