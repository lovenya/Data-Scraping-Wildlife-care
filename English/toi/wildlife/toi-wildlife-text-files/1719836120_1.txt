DEHRADUN: Scores of students including specially-abled and tribal students in over 80 schools of Uttarakhand and HP explored myriad biodiversity during National Wildlife Week, which concluded on Sunday.
Bird, bee, butterfly, mushroom, trees were talked about in walks during the week. Ecologist Raman Kumar and experts at Nature Science Initiative showed over 30 species of birds to students at government schools in Chamoli, Rudraprayag, and Uttarakashi during the initiative held as part of Himalayan charter of 'Wipro earthian program' by Nature Science Initiative, Titli Trust, and Eco-Vigyan foundation.
