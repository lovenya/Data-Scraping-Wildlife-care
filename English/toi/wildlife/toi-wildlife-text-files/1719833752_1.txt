NEW DELHI: Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission has released the UPSSSC Forest Guard, Wildlife Guard Result 2023 today, August 1. Candidates who appeared in the Forest Guard & Wildlife Guard (General Selection) Competitive Examination-2019 can visit the official website at upsssc.gov.in and check their results.

The result of 5630 candidates marked for the Physical Standard Test and Physical Efficiency Test for 655 posts of Forest Guard and Wildlife Guard, 2019, dated February 14, 2023, was released on the website of the Commission.
The Physical Standard Test and Physical Efficiency Test of the said candidates were conducted at Guru Gobind Singh Sports College, Gudamba, Kursi Road, Lucknow from March 20 to April 17, 2023.
