Silchar: A group of local residents found a Bengal slow loris at southern Biskoot in the Longai area of Karimganj district on Wednesday evening. It was handed over to the Karimganj forest department on Thursday, reports BB Goswami.
A southern Biskoot resident, Minu Miya, said on Thursday, the residents contacted social worker Sujan Debroy, following which the animal was handed over to the forest department, Miya said.
