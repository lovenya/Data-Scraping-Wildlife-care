NEW DELHI: The State Disaster Response Force (SDRF) recovered the body of a woman officer and wildlife warden (SDO) on Thursday. The search operation, which began early in the morning near Chilla powerhouse, was carried out as per the orders of Shri Manikant Mishra, Commandant SDRF.

The search for the missing officer started on Monday but was halted at night due to darkness. It resumed on Tuesday and finally proved successful on Thursday with the recovery of the officer's body.
To carry out an intensive search, the SDRF utilized advanced equipment such as sonar and underwater drones. Deep divers from the SDRF used scuba diving techniques to search the depths of the Shakti canal. On Thursday, the water level of the Chila Shakti Canal was lowered, and with the assistance of deep divers, rafts, and motor boats, the body was successfully retrieved.
