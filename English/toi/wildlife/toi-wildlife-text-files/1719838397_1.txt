NEW DELHI: On the occasion of World Wildlife Day on Sunday, Prime Minister Narendra Modi lauded those at the forefront of sustainable practices and supporting wildlife conservation efforts. United Nations World Wildlife Day (WWD) is celebrated every year on March 3. Every year on this day, the unique roles and contributions of wildlife to people's lives and the planet are recognised.

"I also appreciate all those who are at the forefront of sustainable practices, and supporting wildlife conservation efforts," the prime minister said.
