Berhampur: Six fishermen, have been arrested by wildlife personnel for allegedly fishing illegally inside Nalabana, the bird sanctuary in Chilika Lake on Saturday.

The accused were caught by wildlife personnel during patrolling in the sanctuary, said the DFO. They have been booked under different sections of the Wildlife Protection Act 1972, Nayak said.TNN
