Jaipur: Wildlife photographer and conservationist Aditya Dicky Singh, who lived on the fringes of Ranthambore National Park (RNP), passed away on Wednesday due to a suspected cardiac arrest. 
Singh was 57 and is survived by his wife, Poonam Singh, and daughter Nyra.
