TIRUPATI: Wildlife management authorities have identified the leopard responsible for the death of a 6-year-old girl along the Alipiri pedestrian route and have decided to confine it to the SV zoological park in Tirupati.

While several leopards were captured by the forest department officials in the aftermath of this incident, forensic reports confirmed that the fourth leopard captured on August 28, was responsible for the girl's death.
Following a request from the TTD authorities considering the safety of visiting devotees, the wildlife authorities decided to confine the leopard involved in the attack, to the SV zoo in Tirupati.
