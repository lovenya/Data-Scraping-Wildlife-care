Lucknow: The state cabinet on Tuesday approved an increase in the number of posts of forest guards and wildlife guards, While the forest guards will be increased from 2,918 to 3,647, the posts of wildlife guards have been increased from 259 to 266.
On Tuesday, the cabinet did away with the requirement to meet the physical standards for the grade four staff who will be promoted as forest guards and wildlife guards.
 Pay matrix will be applicable to their salary instead of grade pay.
