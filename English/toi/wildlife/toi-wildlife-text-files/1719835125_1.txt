Veteran actors Zeenat Aman and Jackie Shroff have been recognised as the Most Beautiful Vegetarian Celebrities by People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals (PETA) India. Both stars have expressed their love for vegetarian foods. PETA India recognizes them for their compassion towards animals, stating that Aman and Shroff provide sanctuary and kindness to creatures like chickens and dogs.

The recognition highlights the celebrities' efforts in promoting animal welfare, with Aman's warning against the mistreatment of stray dogs and Shroff's commitment to rescuing animals. PETA India acknowledges their exemplary actions, stating that kindness to animals is a beautiful quality. The award-winning actors join the ranks of past winners of the title, including John Abraham, Alia Bhatt, and Amitabh Bachchan.
