NEW DELHI: Three people were rescued after a fire broke out in a house in Lajpat Nagar area of southeast Delhi. The Delhi Fire Services received a call about the blaze at 1:57 am and deployed five fire engines to the scene. 
A senior DFS official said, they rescued three individuals, including two senior citizens, one of whom is differently-abled. The rescued individuals were taken to a nearby hospital to receive treatment for minor burn injuries.
