BHOPAL: A female cheetah roaming in Jaura, Pahargarh, and Kailaras areas of Morena for the past 25 days has been rescued by the state forest department. 
Male cheetah Pawan and female cheetah Veera, had ventured beyond the confines of Kuno National Park in late March.
