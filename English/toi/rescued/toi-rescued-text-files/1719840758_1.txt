LUCKNOW: A heart-wrenching tragedy unfolded in UP's Balrampur as four minor sisters, the youngest aged just seven, drowned in Kunaon river. The incident occurred when the girls, on a visit to their maternal grandmother's home on Bakrid, went for a bath in the river Tuesday evening.
Balrampur SP Keshav Kumar said, "They slipped into deep waters. Despite efforts by locals who rushed to rescue them, they couldn't be saved.The bodies were recovered late in the night with the assistance of divers, police personnel, and local residents."
The girls were identified as Reshma (13), Afsana (11), Guddi (9), and Lalli (7). Their parents Raju and Reshma were in a state of shock.
