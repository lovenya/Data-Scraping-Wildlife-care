BHUBANESWAR: The death toll in the Jharsuguda boat incident rose to five on Saturday. The ferry, carrying 50 people, had capsized in the Mahanadi river near Lakhanpur in Jharsuguda district on Friday afternoon.
The CMO said that all passengers on the boat were from Kharseni in neighboring Chhattisgarh. They had come to Lakhanpur by road and from there had taken the boat to visit a temple at Patharseni in the Ambabhona block of Bargarh district, on the other side of the river.
