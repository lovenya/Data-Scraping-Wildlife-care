Daltonganj: A wounded kite was rescued from a temple located in Lohardaga on Sunday. 
The officer in-charge of the Lohardaga district animal husbandry department, Shrikant Barbude, said, “We were informed about an injured bird that had taken shelter at a Lohardaga temple. We contacted Lohardaga divisional forest officer (DFO) Arvind Kumar to rescue the bird.”
