MUMBAI: A woman riding a two-wheeler at Borivali fainted from low blood pressure, following which the traffic police rushed to her aid, averting a potential road crash. Taking to X (formerly Twitter) on Thursday, the traffic police shared that the woman was travelling to Vile Parle when she fainted on the General Cariappa flyover. Head constable Uma More from the traffic branch along with two other constables offered her lime water and chocolates and called a doctor.
