TIRUPATI: Three students from Palamaner in Andhra Pradesh's Chittoor district were reportedly washed away at the Mahabalipuram beach in Tamil Nadu.
The three students who were washed away were identified as Vijay, a native of Sadum, Prabhu, a resident of Pulicherla and Mounish from Bangarupalem.
