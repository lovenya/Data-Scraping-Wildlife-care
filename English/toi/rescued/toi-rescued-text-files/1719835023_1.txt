NEW DELHI: The CBI on Friday rescued two newborn babies from a house in Delhi's Keshavpuram during searches in connection with child trafficking racket.
The federal agency is interrogating the woman who sold the children and the person who bought them in this case.
CBI has detained some people including a woman and their interrogation is going on.
