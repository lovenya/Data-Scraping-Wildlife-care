BHUBANESWAR: The Directorate of Revenue Intelligence (DRI) has rescued a total of 351 baby Indian tent turtles in Odisha's Cuttack district. 
According to a statement by the DRI, the turtles were being smuggled and were intercepted at the Munguli toll gate. The rescue operation was conducted based on a tip-off received by the DRI.

The Indian tent turtle is a protected species listed in Schedule-I of the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972. The live turtles have been handed over to the forest officials and the arrested individuals have been handed over to the state authorities for further legal action under the provisions of the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972.
