THANE: A 53-year-old man was rescued by local residents after he jumped into a lake in Thane city, Tuesday morning.
Rescue teams said the man – a Khopat resident – attempted suicide inside the Makhmali lake as he was apparently upset with his prolonged ailment and had also soured relationship with his family members.
