MUMBAI: A woman commuter, in her 20s, was rescued by an on-duty railway policeman in the wee hours of Sunday, after she allegedly fell off the train as it was leaving the Bhayander railway station. The woman claimed to have felt dizzy.
The incident was captured on the closed circuit television of platform number 1. When the train halted, a cop, Eknath Mane, was seen helping a disabled man board the northbound general compartment. As the train began leaving the platform, Mane moved away. 
Just then a woman standing at the footboard of the second class ladies compartment (behind the general coach), fell off. She was being dragged along with the train that had not picked up speed.
