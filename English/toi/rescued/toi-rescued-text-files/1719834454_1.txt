GUWAHATI: Personnel of 42 Border Roads Task Force (BRTF) evacuated approximately 70 tourists stranded at Sela Pass in Arunachal Pradesh's Tawang district early on Friday.
Defence PRO Lt Col Mahendra Rawat said heavy snowfall in higher reaches since Feb 22 resulted in traffic disruption and closure of roads: "BRO has gone beyond its mandate of keeping roads open for traffic to carrying out rescue operation to evacuate tourists & locals stranded at the Pass."
