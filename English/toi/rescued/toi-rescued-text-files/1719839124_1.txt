Panaji: A 65-year-old woman who fell into an open well at Ximer, Mapusa, was rescued by firefighters on Wednesday.
The directorate of fire and emergency services received a distress call about a woman, who was identified as Pramila Satpal, falling into a well that was 35 feet deep.
