RAJAHMUNDRY: In a miracle rescue act, fishermen have rescued a woman who attempted suicide by jumping into river Godavari in Rajahmundry on Friday. One Dudala Naga Lakshmi(40) tried to commit suicide due to family problems by jumping from road-cum-rail bridge into river Godavari. The police have received a call and alerted the fishermen in the river. 
The fishermen who were far from the where the woman jumped into the river and reacted very swiftly and rushed to the spot on motor boat in no time.The people who saw the rescue act have appreciated Two Town police and the swimmers who have done a miracle by rescuing the drowning woman. The police gave counseling to the woman and handed over her to her family members.
