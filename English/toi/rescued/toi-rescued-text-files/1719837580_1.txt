Jamshedpur: Though a week has passed since a leopard was first sighted in Adityapur Industrial Area on March 17, the big cat has not been captured yet. However, there’s no let-up in the search and rescue operation, a forest official said.
 The divisional forest officer (DFO) of Seraikela-Kharsawan district, Aditya Narayan said, “Till the animal is either captured or there’s scientific and documented evidence confirming that it has moved out from here, teams will be on the job.”
