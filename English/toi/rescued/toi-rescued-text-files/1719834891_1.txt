At least two persons were killed and several injured after being hit by a train in Jamtara district of Jharkhand on Wednesday, reports Rajesh Kumar Pandey. The death toll might rise after the rescue operation is over. One of the deceased passengers has been identified as Manish Kumar of Katihar district of Bihar whereas the identity of another is not yet to be ascertained.
