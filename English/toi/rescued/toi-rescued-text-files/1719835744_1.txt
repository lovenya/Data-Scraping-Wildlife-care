NEW DELHI: At least four people, including two children, were killed after a massive fire broke out in a residential building Delhi's Shahdara on Thursday morning.
"We received a call at around 5.20am about a major fire at Shastri Nagar, near Geeta Colony. We informed the Delhi fire services immediately. A police team, four fire tenders, ambulances and PCR vans were rushed to the spot," a senior police officer said.
