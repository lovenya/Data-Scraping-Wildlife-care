NEW DELHI: Bulgarian President Rumen Radev Monday thanked PM Narendra Modi for Indian Navy’s anti-piracy operation that saw it successfully rescuing seven Bulgarians who were part of a crew that was being held hostage by pirates aboard a hijacked ship.

Bulgaria’s deputy PM Mariya Gabriel had also expressed the same sentiment in a post on X on Sunday. “I express my gratitude to the Indian Navy for the successful operation to rescue the hijacked vessel Ruen and its crew members, including 7 BG nationals. Thank you for support and great effort. We continue to work together to protect lives of the crew,” said Gabriel, who is also the minister of foreign affairs.
