NOIDA: A businessman who was abducted from Ballabgarh in Haryana, was rescued by the Greater Noida Police on Tuesday morning. The businessman was abducted by four criminals on Monday night.
The abduction took place while the man was on his way to Delhi.

ADCP, Greater Noida, Ashok Kumar says "A businessman from Ballabgarh, Haryana was abducted last night by 4 people when he was travelling to Delhi. The kidnappers kept driving him to several places. After seeing Police on the road, the kidnappers left the car and ran away. After which the person was rescued and is being sent back."
