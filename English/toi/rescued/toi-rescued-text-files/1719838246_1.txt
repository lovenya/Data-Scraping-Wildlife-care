AGRA: Four friends drowned after they went to take a dip in Ganga canal in Mainpuri district on Saturday, police said, adding that local villagers jumped into the canal and saved one of them while the other three are yet to be found, reports Mohammad Dilshad.
DSP, Mainpuri, Sunil Kumar told TOI, “ A team of divers and local police are in a search operation.The lone survivor is doing well. They were all from the same locality in Kusmara and were graduating from various colleges. Their families have been informed.”
Vishnu Pandey (18), Ankit Dixit (19), Yash Gupta (18), and Golu Gupta (19) had gone for a dip under the bridge across Chhoti Ganga canal near Raihar village and started drowning, police added.
