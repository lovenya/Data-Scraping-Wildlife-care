NEW DELHI: A passenger taxi rolled down a deep gorge on the Jammu-Srinagar national highway on Friday.
The mishap occurred near Battery Chashma in the Ramban area. 
Police, SDRF and civil QRT Ramban reached the spot and a rescue operation is under way.
