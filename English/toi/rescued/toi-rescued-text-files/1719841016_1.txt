MANGALURU: Four members of a family tragically lost their lives when a house wall collapsed onto a neighbouring house at Kuthar Madani Nagara in the Ullal police station limits on Wednesday morning. Preliminary information suggests that the victims are Yasir, his wife Mariyamma, and their children Riyana and Rifan. 
The Ullal police, Fire and Emergency Services Personnel, and locals assisted in the rescue operation.Speaker of the legislative assembly and MLA UT Khader is expected to visit the spot. Khader is scheduled to hold a review meeting of various departments later in the day. A large number of people gathered at the spot.
