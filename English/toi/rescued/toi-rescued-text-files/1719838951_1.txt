PATNA: Two persons lost their lives when a boat overturned in the Ganga River near Mahavir Tola ghat at Maner, located in the Patna district on Sunday morning.
According to the police, two individuals, Vijendra Kumar Rai (59) and Ranvir Kumar (34), both hailing from Gauraiyasthan Naga Toli under Maner police station area, are feared to have drowned.
