NASHIK: Three State Disaster Response Force (SDRF) jawans on mission to trace a youth missing in a river drowned as their boat capsized in Maharashtra's Ahmednagar on Thursday. A local accompanying the team is missing.
Two other jawans were rescued from the Pravara river Sugaon (Budruk) village of Ahmednagar's Akole taluka. "Three jawans were pulled out but the doctors declared them brought dead," said Akole tehsildar Siddharth More.
