Kolkata: Members of West Bengal Radio Club, who had set up a camp to help accident victims on Monday, ended up rescuing passengers stuck inside Kanchanjungha Express. Kajal Roy and Shekhar Debnath, who had earlier helped Sikkim flood victims, led the operations. “They started bringing injured passengers out and shifted them to hospitals. They could send eight to hospitals,” said Ambarish Nag Biswas, founder of the club.tnn
