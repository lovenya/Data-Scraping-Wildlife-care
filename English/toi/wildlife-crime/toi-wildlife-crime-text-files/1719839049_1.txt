NEW DELHI: A snake charmer was sent to judicial custody at Tihar Jail on Sunday after he was caught with an Indian cobra in the southern ridge.
The man was apprehended by the south division of the forest and wildlife department and a case was registered at Mehrauli police station. 
The man was produced before the court, citing a violation of the Wildlife Protection Act, 1972 and the snake was released at the Asola Bhatti Wildlife Sanctuary.
