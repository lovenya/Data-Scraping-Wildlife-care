NASHIK: Forest department officials on Monday raided a dry fruit and medicinal herbs shop in the Teli lane area of Nashik city and recovered animal body parts from the outlet. They have also booked the owner of the shop under sections of the Wildlife Protection Act. 
Acting on a tip-off, a squad of forest officials raided the shop. Forest officials said that the animal body parts include those of monitor lizards, pieces of broken horns, snake skins, sea fan, leather rings, etc.The officials are in the process of identifying the other body parts found at the shop.
“Since possessing body parts of animals is a violation of the Wildlife Protection Act, we have registered a crime against the owner of the shop,” forest officials said.
Forest department officials further said that they are investigating the case.
