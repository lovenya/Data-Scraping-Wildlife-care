TRICHY: Wildlife Crime Control Bureau (WCCB) personnel seized more than 300 parakeets from two men here on Thursday night. 
Parakeet is a species protected under Schedule IV of the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972. 
Posing as potential buyers, WCCB personnel approached two men, who were part of a racket selling parakeets, in Kuruvikaran Street near Palakkarai.
