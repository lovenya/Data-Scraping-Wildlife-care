Kolhapur: The forest department in Karad has detained a poacher for hunting a monitor lizard to consume its meat.
The incident took place near the Shenoli village of Satara’s Karad tehsil. The forest officials raid the house of the accused. Monitor lizard falls under schedule 1 part 1 of the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972.

Our team then raided the house of the accused at Gopalnagar within the jurisdiction of the Shenoli station. We found the dead monitor lizard and the accused at his home.
The accused has been detained in a case of poaching and the dead lizard has been seized. “Hunting and handling of monitor lizards is prohibited by law and invites imprisonment of up to seven years and a fine of up to Rs 10,000,” added Bhate.
