BENGALURU: Rana, the fiery German Shepherd at Bandipur Tiger Reserve died of age-related sickness on Tuesday morning. 
Inducted into the ranks of Karnataka forest department in 2017, Rana soon became the most sought after dog tracing tiger poachers and tracking man-eater tigers. 
The 10-year-old earned the sobriquet ‘The Hunter’ for detecting over 10 wildlife cases and partially helping in 5 to 6 cases in the past few years, 
Several adjoining states like Tamil Nadu and Kerala had sought Rana's assistance in tracking tigers and detecting wildlife crimes.Senior forest officials from Bandipura told TOI that Rana will be cremated with state honours.
