RUDRAPUR: The Kumaon branch of Uttarakhand Special Task Force (STF), in collaboration with the Wildlife Crime Control Bureau (WCCB), Delhi, apprehended a wildlife smuggler from Udham Singh Nagar on Saturday.
The accused, Purne Vishwkarma alias Hariom, a resident of Doti district in Nepal, was attempting to transport two musk pods from Nepal into India when he was arrested on Khatima-Pilibhit road in the district.
The operation was led by STF circle officer (CO) of Kumaon region, Sumit Pandey, along with a joint team comprising WCCB Delhi and Terai East Forest Division, Haldwani.
“We received a tip leading to the arrest of Vishwkarma who confessed to planning to sell the two recovered musk pods to an individual named Puran in Haryana. The arrested smuggler, along with Puran, had hunted two Alpine musk deers in Nepal and extracted their musk,” said CO Pandey. A case has been registered against the smuggler by the forest department in Khatima.
Alpine musk deer, the state animal of Uttarakhand, holds protected status under the Wildlife Protection Act.
