MUMBAI: A 36-year-old man was arrested by the Mumbai crime branch with skin of black buck at Ghatkopar Link Road on Wednesday. 
The arrested accused identified as John Sundarrao Yesamala, is a Sewree resident and is unemployed. According to police inspector Manish Shridhankar, the accused had brought the black buck from Kalyan and wanted to sell it for Rs 10 lakh.
