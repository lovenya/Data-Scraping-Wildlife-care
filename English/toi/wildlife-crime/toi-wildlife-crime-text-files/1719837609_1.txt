AURANGABAD: Activists and citizens want law enforcement agencies to take stricter action against users of the banned nylon manja. 
Honorary wildlife warden for Aurangabad Kishor Pathak said only intense action could serve as a deterrent. “We demand action against users of nylon manja similar to the action against narcotics users,” he said.

Pointing out that the use of nylon manja is bound to soar during the Sankranti, Gajanan Kulkarni, another resident, said authorities must not wait for the harmful kite strings to take a higher toll on the lives of humans and birds among others.
