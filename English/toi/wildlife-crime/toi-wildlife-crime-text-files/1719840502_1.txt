Chennai: Central Wildlife Crime Control Bureau officials and state forest department staff on Monday seized a tiger skin and arrested four people in Nagercoil. The tiger skin, a protected item under the Wildlife Protection Act, belonged to a Coimbatore resident. 
Kripan Shankar, regional deputy director, wildlife crime control bureau, Chennai, told TOI, “We work under the government of India and most of our operations are undertaken with TN forest department.After the arrest, we hand over the case to the district forest official incharge.” tnn
