CUDDALORE: The Cuddalore and Chennai ranges of Tamil Nadu Forest and Wildlife Crime Control Bureau conducted a joint raid at a house of an official of NLC India Limited and seized wildlife crime items from his possession. 
The seized items are a spotted deer skin, four deer antlers, two live star tortoises and a parakeet. The bureau officials arrested P Sridhar, 54, a depiity executive engineer at NLCIL.
