BHUBANESWAR: The court of judicial magistrate of first class at Kantamal in Boudh district sentenced a man to three years of rigorous imprisonment (RI) on Friday after holding him guilty for smuggling a leopard skin, which was recovered from him on April 28, 2021, by the special task force (STF) of state crime branch.
The convict was identified as Mahendra Bhaina of Badalguda village in Boudh district.According to the STF’s inspector general J N Pankaj, this is the first wildlife case in Odisha where the maximum punishment of three years has been awarded to an accused under the Wildlife Protection Act. “During investigation, we sent the seized leopard skin to the Wildlife Institute of India, Dehradun, for biological/chemical examination,” Pankaj said.tnn
