Kolkata: Based on intelligence developed by Wildlife Crime Control Bureau unit in Siliguri, the agency officials, along with Ghospukur forest range officers, seized 5kg of dry seahorse and one motorbike near Naxalbari around 3pm on Tuesday. One person, a resident of Islampur, has also been arrested. TNN
