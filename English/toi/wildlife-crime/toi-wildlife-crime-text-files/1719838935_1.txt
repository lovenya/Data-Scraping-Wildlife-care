KOLHAPUR: The forest department on Friday carried out a surprise raid on the house of a former police patil and seized three claws of a wild animal, suspected to be a tiger or leopard, besides the liver of a wild animal and a gun with 13 live cartridges.

Assistant conservator of forest Navnath Kamble said, “Based on information we received, we raided Vaskar’s house at Mouje Shivdav in Bhudargad taluka of Kolhapur in the afternoon. The three animal claws were hidden in a turmeric powder jar in the kitchen and the liver in the fridge.”
Department sources said Vaskar had sold two claws of a wild animal earlier. He has also been hunting illegally in the forest area using his jeep and gun, the sources added.
