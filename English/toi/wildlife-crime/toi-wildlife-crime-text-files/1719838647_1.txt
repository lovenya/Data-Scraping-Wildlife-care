GUWAHATI: Ten people have been arrested with four live pangolins, 43 dead tokay gecko, 44.28 kg pangolin scale and two elephant tusks in two different operations on April 19 and 20, forest officials said on Thursday.
Acting on a tip-off of the Wildlife Crime Control Bureau (WCCB), the raid was conducted at the residence of Victorious Masharing of Khlichumsotem village in Meghalaya’s Ri-Bhoi.

Based on the statement of the arrested accused, another operation was conducted at Nongpoh of Ri-Bhoi on April 20 and two elephant tusks were found and two persons were arrested.
