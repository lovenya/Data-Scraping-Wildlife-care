Coimbatore: Sirumugai forest range officers on Saturday arrested two men for poaching a spotted deer in Pethikuttai reserve forest. 
A forest officer identified the accused as B Gandhi, 38, of Amman Pudur, and R Kannan, 37, of Koothamandi near Sirumugai.

During inquiry, they confessed that they poached the deer using the snare.”
The two were subsequently booked under the Wildlife Protection Act and arrested. “They were produced before a judicial magistrate, who remanded them in judicial custody,” the officer said.
