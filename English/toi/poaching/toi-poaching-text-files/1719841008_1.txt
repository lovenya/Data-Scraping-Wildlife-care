Guwahati: In the first incident of rhino poaching in the state this year, a female rhino was poached at Kaziranga National Park and Tiger Reserve (KNPTR), park officials said on Monday.
The horn of the dead rhino has been found missing, the KNPTR said in a statement.

While the post mortem report is awaited, the forest and police departments have intensified search operation to nab the poachers involved. 
Kaziranga achieved zero rhino poaching record in 2022, which was the first such instance in a calendar year since 1977. But last year, one rhino was poached, despite best efforts in surveillance.
