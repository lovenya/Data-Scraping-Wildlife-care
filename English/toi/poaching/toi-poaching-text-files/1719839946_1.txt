TIRUPATI: The red sanders anti-smuggling task force sleuths arrested seven people on charges of attempting to poach inside the Seshachalam forests near Jillelamanda beat section in Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh.

The teams, which went into the forest area in search of red sanders smugglers, accidentally came across a gang of poachers from Annamayya district.
The police parties surrounded the poachers' gang and took them into custody. 
A country-made weapon, along with some pellets and other materials, were seized from the possession of the accused. 
The gang tried to hunt down a stag before the task force personnel surrounded them and took them into custody.
