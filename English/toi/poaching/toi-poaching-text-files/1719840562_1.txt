Ramanathapuram: The Sri Lankan navy detained six Tamil Nadu fishermen on charges of poaching in the territorial waters of the island nation on Monday night. The navy detained their two fishing trawlers too. 
The fishermen sailed from Rameswaram on Monday evening. The fishing boats of A Isaac and S Arockiadas of Thangachimadam were detained. Isaac and Arockiadas were among the six detained fishermen.The other crew members are A Siserian, S Samathanababu, M Nishanth and Murugesan.They were produced before a court in Oorkavalthurai and remanded in judicial custody. The fishermen were later lodged in Jaffna prison. 
“We have decided to cut short the fishing hours from 24 to 18 hours to reduce our encounter with the Lankan navy.”“But our fishermen were held as soon as they started fishing,” said fishermen leader P Jesuraja.
