TRICHY: Police on Sunday arrested five people for allegedly poaching deer in Tamil Nadu’s Trichy district. 
The police seized a mini-van and two unlicensed rifles used in the hunting by the accused gang.
The police said a special team was engaged in vehicular inspection from Perambalur to Settikulam in the early hours of Sunday.

The occupants of the van were identified as Manikandan, 24, Govindan, 33, Karthik 19 and Ramachandran, 30, all residents of Perambalur. 
The accused include a juvenile. 
The poachers confessed that they hunted the deer using country-made rifles in Edumalai reserved forest area. 
Police said the accused and the remains of the poached deer were handed over to the forest department officials for further legal action.
