RAIPUR: The Chhattisgarh anti-poaching team in Udanti Sitanadi Tiger Reserve in Gariaband in joint operation with neighbouring Maharashtra forest department has nabbed two persons with a tiger skin on a motor bike in wee hours on Thursday. 
Based on specific inputs that smugglers were in attempt of trading a tiger skin in Maharashtra’s Etapalli region, bordering Chhattisgarh, Varun Jain, the deputy director of Udanti Sitanadi approached Bhamragarh divisional forest officer Shailesh Meena.
An operation was immediately chalked out and raid was conducted with seizure of skin and arrest of two accused identified as Shyam Lal Naraoti and Amjad Pathan, while few others managed to escape.
They were taken in custody by Etapalli forest division for further investigations. Earlier, another joint operation had to be called off after the poachers got info about the raid and they fled.
