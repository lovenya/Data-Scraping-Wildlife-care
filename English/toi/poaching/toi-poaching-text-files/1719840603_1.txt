Imphal: At least five hog deer were seized along with 200 animal trapping nets by Arambai Tenggol (AT) members and the state forest department during a joint three-day anti-poaching drive at Keibul Lamjao National Park (KNLP) in Bishnupur district.
They also found nine carcasses of the endangered animals during the drive which ended on Sunday.
