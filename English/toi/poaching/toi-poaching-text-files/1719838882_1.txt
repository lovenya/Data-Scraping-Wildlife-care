CHANDRAPUR: In a joint operation, a team of foresters from Jiwti range along with their counterparts from Telangana recovered tiger parts hidden in the forest near Asifabad by poachers arrested with tiger skin recently. 
Six members of the inter-state gang of poachers were arrested with tiger skin from Pataguda village in Jiwti taluka on April 2.
