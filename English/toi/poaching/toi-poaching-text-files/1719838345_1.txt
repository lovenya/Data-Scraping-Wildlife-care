NAGPUR: Navegaon-Nagzira Tiger Reserve (NNTR) officials arrested three persons and seized seven nails and other body parts in the tiger poaching case inside Koka wildlife sanctuary in Bhandara district.
Adult tiger (T-13) was found dead in Katang nala in compartment number 180 on March 26, 2023.
