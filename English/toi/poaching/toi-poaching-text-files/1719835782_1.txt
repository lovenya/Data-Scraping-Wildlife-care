Jaisalmer: Poaching of migratory birds in desert national parks and other areas is increasing and many poachers are supplying the poached birds to hotels, etc. A poacher on Thursday poached a grey francolin bird with a gun in Khabha area of Khuhadi police station of Jaisalmer. The photo of the poacher with gun became viral. In this regard, bird expert and Akhil Bharatiya Jeev Raksha Vishnoi Sabha Jaisalmer district president Radheyshyam Pemani gave a written complaint after which the forest department arrested the poacher and seized his gun on Saturday. Pemani said that a poacher in the Khabha area poached a grey francolin bird and was roaming with the gun in his hand. tnn
