MAHABUBABAD: A 12-member poacher gang that killed a wild bear a week ago were arrested on Tuesday. 
The incident took place Jangalapalli village in Gangaram mandal of Mahabubabad last Monday. 
Owing to the Medaram Jatara officials the incident came to light late.
