Erode: Officials from the Bhavanisagar forest range arrested two men on Wednesday for poaching two spotted deer. The accused were identified as N Othichamy, 58 and I Dinesh Kumar, 46, from Bhavanisagar. A team led by range officer S Sivakumar nabbed the duo from Varappallam forest after receiving a tip-off. “"The men used snares to catch deer on Tuesday night.They have confessed to the crime,” the officer said.
