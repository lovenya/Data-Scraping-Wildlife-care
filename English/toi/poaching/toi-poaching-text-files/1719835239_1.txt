COLOMBO: The Sri Lankan Navy has arrested 43 Indian fishermen and seized six trawlers for allegedly poaching in the country's territorial waters, an official statement said on Sunday.
The Navy arrested the fishermen in seas Southeast of the Delft Island in Jaffna on Saturday.
