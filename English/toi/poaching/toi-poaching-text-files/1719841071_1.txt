Richie Mehta's directorial crime drama series ‘Poacher’, featuring Nimisha Sajayan, Roshan Mathew, and Dibyendu Bhattacharya, is receiving an overwhelming response from across the globe. The gripping series sheds light on the harsh realities of elephant poaching.
