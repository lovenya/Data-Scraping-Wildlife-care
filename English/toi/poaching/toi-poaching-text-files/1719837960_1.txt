NAGPUR: The Butibori forest range officials have arrested four persons for poaching a nilgai and consuming its meat. ACF Narendra Chandewar said, “Acting on a tip-off that a nilgai was hunted and its flesh consumed, we rounded up four persons from Kharda and Tembhri villages.”
