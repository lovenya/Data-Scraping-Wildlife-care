BHOPAL: Madhya Pradesh high court has issued arrest warrants against 11 people, who were acquitted recently by a special court in Sagar district in a high profile poaching case.
The state government had moved high court, challenging the acquittal of 11 of 13 people sentenced to seven years' jail by a trial court for allegedly poaching and smuggling endangered pangolins and turtles. It reduced sentences of two others by three years.
Advocate Pradeep Gupta appeared for the state while advocate R N Dwivedi appeared before the court and undertook to file power on behalf of respondents.Judge Rajeev Kumar Dubey ordered bailable warrants against the 11 on Rs 2,000 each to secure their presence in court on the next date of hearing.
