Jaisalmer: Unidentified persons poached a chinkara at Karmooro Meghwal ki Dhani near Bheekhodai town in Phalsoond police station area of Jaisalmer on Friday night. Hearing the firing sound, villagers came running with torches but the poachers fled the spot by then. The chinkara died on the spot.
