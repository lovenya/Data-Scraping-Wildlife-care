Raipur: Chhattisgarh Forest Department has so far arrested 39 persons for poaching in Indravati Tiger Reserve, Bijapur, and sent them on judicial remand. Seven other accused are still absconding. On July 3, 2023, the forest team arrested nine persons. The gang is involved in the poaching of wildlife such as tigers, sloth bears and turtles, posing a grave threat to the wildlife.
The Chhattisgarh forest department found a link of 14 accused to Maharashtra, and apprehended them from Bhandara, Gondia and Chandrapur districts of Maharashtra, for their involvement in the tiger poaching incident at Kosatola village, Salekasa, Chhattisgarh, said Dhammashil Ganvir, deputy director of Indravati Tiger Reserve.
