Jaisalmer: A deer was poached in the canal area of Jaisalmer district around 7-8 days ago and the forest department is yet to take any action in the matter. 
The Vishnoi community and other villagers on Saturday staged a sit-in outside the office of Nehdai forest department. They alleged that the forest department is trying to protect the accused. 
The accused poachers and others are threatening the eyewitnesses and trying to suppress the case.Wildlife enthusiast Mahipal Vishnoi said that till the forest department takes action against the poachers, the dharna will continue. A large number of villagers, wildlife enthusiasts and others are gathering outside the dharna spot.
