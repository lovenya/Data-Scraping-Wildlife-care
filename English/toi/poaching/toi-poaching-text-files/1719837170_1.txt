KHARGONE: The body of a leopard was found floating in a river with some of its parts missing, indicating that the wild cat was a victim of poaching in Bistan forest area of Madhya Pradesh's Khargone district.
SDO forest Garvit Gangwar told TOI that villagers on Tuesday evening informed about the body of a leopard found in Kunda river under a bridge between forest village Pipaljhopa and Raisagar.

"Its paws, tail, and whiskers were missing," he said.
A postmortem examination is being conducted by a team of veterinary doctors at Pipaljhopa to know the cause of death.
DFO Khargone Prashant Singh said, "We are not ruling out possibilities of poaching, but it can be ascertained after postmortem examination and further investigation".
