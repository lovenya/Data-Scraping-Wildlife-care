AHMEDABAD: The state government on Monday admitted in a tabled reply in the assembly that eight incidents of poaching have taken place in Gir sanctuary since 2018. This was stated in reply to a question raised by Gandhinagar North MLA CJ Chavda. However, the government in the reply has not mentioned which animals were poached in these incidents.
