Jaisalmer: A chinkara which was caught in a fence was poached in Sadia village in Jaisalmer district on Thursday night. 
Police have arrested two poachers and seized parts of a chinkara, one alive chinkara, sharp weapons and a Bolero vehicle. Later, they were handed over to the forest department.
