Silchar: Nineteeen women of the Barak Valley, who were taken to Ghaziabad by a placement agency promising jobs of nurses, fled and came back to Silchar after they allegedly realized that they have been duped and trafficked. 
Accoding to reports, 11 more women from the Barak Valley are believed to be still trapped there. The escapees informed about the matter to Cachar SP Numal Mahatta on Monday and sought action against the agency.
