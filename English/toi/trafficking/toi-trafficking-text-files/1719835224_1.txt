Raipur: The Railway Protection Force (RPF) arrested two drug traffickers on Saturday, seizing 20 kilograms and 500 grams of ganja from them at the Raipur railway station. The estimated value of the confiscated ganja is around Rs 2.5 lakh. 
The suspects have been identified as Sarjesh Singh, 20, a resident of Madro village, and Prashant Dubey, 19, hailing from Kadhihai village in the Rewa district of Madhya Pradesh.

Based on information, the suspects were cornered and captured near the toilet meant for physically-challenged on platform number five-six of the Raipur railway, and upon checking two bags they had, illegal substances were found inside them. Further investigation revealed that the bags contained illicit substances, specifically ganja.

During interrogation, they admitted to the transportation of the seized ganja through the railway route to Dabhaura, located within their home district, with the intention of selling it.
After the necessary actions were taken, the seized ganja, along with all relevant documents, was handed over to the Raipur Government Railway Police station for further investigation. A case was registered against both under the NDPS Act.
