HYDERABAD: Cyberabad police launched a massive surveillance programme against 142 human trafficking offenders identified across all five zones within their commissionerate. 
The initiative, which began on July 1, will cover Balanagar, Madhapur, Rajendranagar, Shamshabad and Medchal zones.
Cops said that all activities of organisers, sub-organisers or middlemen in sex rackets, or those involved in the illegal transportation of girls for immoral sexual exploitation are being monitored.
