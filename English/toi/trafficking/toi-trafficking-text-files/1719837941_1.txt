NEW DELHI: Two minors have been rescued as four people, including a man and his wife, have been arrested by the Delhi Police from Andhra Pradesh for their alleged involvement in trafficking of two girls, officials said on Wednesday.

"We have arrested four people from Andhra Pradesh's Anantapur for allegedly trafficking two minor girls from Delhi," PTI quoted deputy commissioner of police (Shahdara) Rohit Meena as saying.
The victims were held in captivity and forced to engage in illicit acts, the DCP said.
He said a complaint was received in this regard on August 21 at the Seemapuri police station.
"We got support from Chennai and Andhra Pradesh police to arrest these accused," DCP Meena said.
