Ghaziabad: A 45-year-old woman was arrested for allegedly smuggling cannabis worth Rs 3.6 crore on Thursday. 
According to the police, Sitara, a Loni resident was caught with the 9kg charas. Police are trying to find out where the woman was smuggling the Charas to Delhi-NCR.
