VISAKHAPATNAM: The Directorate of Revenue Intelligence (DRI) on Tuesday seized a leopard skin and nabbed four persons in Vizag city. The officers of DRI developed specific intelligence that a gang of traffickers were poaching leopards and selling the skin for profit.

The leopard skin and the vehicles used by the gang were seized and the four persons were immediately apprehended.
As a testimony to enhanced inter-agency coordination, the Leopard skin was immediately handed over to the Andhra Pradesh State Forest officials.
