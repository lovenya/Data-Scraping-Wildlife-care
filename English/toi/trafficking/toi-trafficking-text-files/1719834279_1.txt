THANE: The Anti Human Trafficking Cell (AHTC) of the Thane police have arrested a 21-year-old pimp who reportedly operated an inter-state flesh trade racket. The cops have also rescued five women.

The police said that they found five other women at the spot who were rescued from the flesh trade. During the investigation it transpired that the accused woman was actively involved in the sex racket and would supply the girls from various metro and semi metro cities and other tourist places, as well as from Goa, the police said.
“She would pick women especially those working as side dancers in Bollywood movies at some events or dance shows and would lure them into earning quick money. Once convinced, she would then send their photos to the customers and accordingly the women were supplied,” said the police officer.
