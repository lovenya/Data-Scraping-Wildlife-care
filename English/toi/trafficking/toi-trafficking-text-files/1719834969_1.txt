Lucknow: Acting on the zero tolerance policy against criminals, the Barabanki police seized property worth Rs 16.1 crore belonging to a history-sheeter, Munawwar Raza, on Sunday. 
Superintendent of police, Barabanki, Dinesh Kumar Singh said that action against the history-sheeter came under a campaign being run against criminals who commit organized crime in the district.The accused indulged in drug trafficking and extortion, he said. “Overall 12 immovable properties belonging to the accused and bank accounts were attached which were the proceeds of criminal activities,” said Singh.
