DIBRUGARH: Three women were arrested with 348gms of heroin valued at Rs 1.5 crore in Dibrugarh district on Friday, police said.
They were carrying the drug consignment from Dimapur (Nagaland) via Duliajan (Assam) to Arunachal Pradesh when police intercepted them in the Joypur area of the district. The drugs were packed in 29 soap cases.
