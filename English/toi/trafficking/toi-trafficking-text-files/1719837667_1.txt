NEW DELHI: Delhi Police Special Cell arrested a 48-year-old drug trafficker in Punjab on Monday. 
Investigation revealed that he had supplied more than 500 kg of opium and heroin in Delhi, Punjab, Assam, and Uttar Pradesh and used to transport the drugs by concealing them in secret cavities created in cars and trucks.
