MUMBAI: Two men were arrested by the Anti-Narcotics Cell (ANC) of the Mumbai Police with 230 gm of mephedrone worth Rs 46.31 lakh. 
The duo was picked up between Friday night and early on Saturday from Marol in Andheri East.
