MOTIHARI: In a joint operation conducted by the anti-human trafficking unit of SSB, police and local administration, eight Scheduled Tribe (ST) girls and three boys from Jharkhand and West Bengal were rescued from a house at Raxaul in East Champaran district on Monday.

Raxaul SDPO Dhirendra Kumar said one Premlata Marandi of Kathikund village in Dumaka district of Jharkhand had lodged an FIR in this connection. Police arrested two persons, Bikki Marandi and Parwati Marandi of Jamtara in Jharkhand, and raids are being conducted to arrest Anamul Ansari and Vikash Sharma of Raxaul. He said one out of eight girls belonged to West Bengal.
