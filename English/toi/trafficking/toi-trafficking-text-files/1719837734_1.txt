KOLKATA: The Kolkata Police arrested a couple for allegedly selling their newborn granddaughter for Rs 30,000, officials reported on Thursday.
The 23-day-old newborn baby was rescued from Narendrapur in the South 24 Parganas district, and the couple was apprehended in Gaya, Bihar, where they had fled to after selling her.
