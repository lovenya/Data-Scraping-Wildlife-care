Mangaluru: Anti-Human Trafficking Club of St Philomena College, Puttur launched a series of workshops in partnership with legal bodies, to raise awareness on World Anti-Human Trafficking Day. A series of workshops aimed at creating awareness among approximately 1,000 students across various identified schools were organised.
