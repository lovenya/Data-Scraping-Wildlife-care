JAMMU: In a resolute crackdown against drug trafficking and substance abuse, Jammu police achieved a major breakthrough over the weekend, apprehending four narcotics smugglers, including two females from Punjab.

The successful operations were led by diligent teams of officers from various police stations, each working meticulously to bring the smugglers to justice. The spokesperson for the Jammu police issued a statement detailing the arrests and the substantial amount of illicit substances that were seized.
In the first operation, officers from police post Narwal, under the leadership of SHO PS Bahu Fort inspector Shiv Dev Singh, apprehended a suspect identified as Raj Kumar from Upper Krishna Nagar, Jammu. During a thorough search of his belongings, a staggering 4,500 Spam M+ psychotropic capsules were discovered in his possession, underscoring the magnitude of the illicit activity he was involved in.
