Surat: The special operation group of Valsad police seized 81 kg poppy capsules and their powder worth Rs 2.42 lakh from Gundlav village of Valsad district on Saturday. The contraband was stored in two rented rooms.

Bishnoi is on the run and his family was also not found at the residence.
Neighbours told police that Bishnoi left with his family, locking the house a few days ago.
 He lived in Gundlav at a different location from the place where the poppy was recovered.
 Police suspect that he sold it to other drug traffickers and individual buyers. He rented the rooms from its owner Jitendra Prajapati around a year ago.
Based on specific information, police raided the house on Friday evening. Police got the recovered product tested in the Forensic Science Laboratory which confirmed that it was poppy.
“Poppy is popular among migrants from Rajasthan. They consume it in traditional methods,” said a police officer.
 Police did not find any criminal background of Bishnoi. Police have launched a search for him and identified his associates.
