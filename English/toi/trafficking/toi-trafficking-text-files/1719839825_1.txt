MUMBAI: Police arrested two individuals in Navi Mumbai after seizing cannabis worth Rs 55.68 lakh. 
Acting on a tip-off, the Navi Mumbai police's Anti-Narcotics Cell set up a trap in Panvel. The accused, both from Mumbai, were caught with 1.85 kg of charas.
