Jaipur: The Jaipur police on Tuesday arrested two arms traffickers who were involved in supplying weapons through Instagram. 
Additional police commissioner Kailash Chandra Bishnoi said that the police arrested the accused idenitfied as Surendra Sain, a resident of Govindgarh near Jaipur, and the second accused Mastram Meena is from Kota.
Bishnoi said that during the operation, they seized eight pistols, nine magazines, and 19 cartridges. 
The police stated that during interrogation, the accused confessed to purchasing weapons for Rs 15,000 and selling them in the market for Rs 30,000.
