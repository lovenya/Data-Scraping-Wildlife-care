Rourkela: A couple from Jharkhand, accused of human trafficking, were nabbed by Railway Protection Force here on Tuesday night. They were handed over to Gumla police in Jharkhand on Wednesday. The accused were identified Samim Ansari (28) and Munni Khatoon (33) of Simdega district of Jharkhand.
