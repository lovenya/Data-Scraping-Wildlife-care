Kolkata: The Bengal CID arrested the two main traffickers behind the sale of a woman (24) from Diamond Harbour to a brothel in New Delhi, cops said.
The accused, Samsul Sk alias Saddam and Abadulla Mondal, were produced before the ACJM court in Diamond Harbour, said SS (headquarters) Indra Chakraborty. TNN
