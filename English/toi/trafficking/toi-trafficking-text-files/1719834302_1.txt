Chandigarh: Police have arrested two people after 55 gram of heroin was allegedly recovered from them in separate instances. Kuldeep Sharma (23), a resident of Kaithal, Haryana, was caught with 39.17 gram of heroin from near the Government College for Girls, Sector 11, said cops. Kashish Khurana (25), a resident of Sector 23, was caught with 19 gram of heroin from near the Children Traffic Park in Sector 23.
