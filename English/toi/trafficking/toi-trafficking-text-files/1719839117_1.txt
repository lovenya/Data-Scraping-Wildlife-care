Colva: Citing shoddy investigation, additional sessions court judge Pooja Kavlekar has acquitted a man facing trial under the Immoral Traffic (Prevention) Act. Anwar Kandgal was arrested in November 2017 following a raid at the Margao railway station. The prosecution claimed that Kandgal was transporting victims in his car.
