Mohali: Police have booked two women and a man on charges of forcing a girl into immoral trafficking. Cops said an 18-year-old girl has been rescued from the clutches of her mother, maternal aunt and her mother’s lover. Police have arrested her mother and her partner. A court on Thursday remanded them to one-day police custody. The girl alleged her mother and her aunt with the connivance of her mother’s lover were forcing her into immoral trafficking.She alleged that her elder sister was also forced into the illegal sex trade.
