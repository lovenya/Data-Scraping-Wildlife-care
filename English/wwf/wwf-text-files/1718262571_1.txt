Diane Monet grew up "loving everything that breathed." She developed a passion for animals at a young age, and became a WWF member in 1995. She has further comitted herself to conserving animals by joining WWF's Legacy Circle, a group of people who want to make a lasting impact on saving the world's most threatened species by including WWF in their estate plans.
