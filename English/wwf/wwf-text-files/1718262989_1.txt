Moving toward a sustainable fishery takes commitment from all sides: Fishers must reduce their impact on the waters they fish; managers must ensure the catch is properly monitored; and policy makers must put, and keep, sufficient management measures in place. In Ecuador, WWF is working to improve the artisanal longline mahi mahi fishery, which exports fresh fish primarily to buyers in the US. Learn more about sustainable seafood.
