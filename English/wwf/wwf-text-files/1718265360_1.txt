On July 6, 2016, the US Fish and Wildlife Service (USFWS) imposed a “near-total ban” on imports, exports, and domestic trade of African elephant ivory. A year later, TRAFFIC, WWF, and the International Fund for Animal Welfare released a study that established a baseline picture of that market in the US.
