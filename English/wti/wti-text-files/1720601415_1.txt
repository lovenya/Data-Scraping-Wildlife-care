Bokakhat (Assam): The Police patrolling team arrested five alleged rhino poachers at about 9.30 pm on August 30 after receiving a tip off. The men were arrested in front of the Bokakhat police station on the highway near Kaziranga national Park in the north east Indian Assam state. KNP is reeling under a poaching outbreak and at least 12 rhinos have been killed in the past few months.
