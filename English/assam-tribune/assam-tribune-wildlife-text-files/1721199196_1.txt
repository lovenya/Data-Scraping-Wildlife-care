JAGIROAD, Oct 3 - Along with other parts of the country, the 64th Wildlife Week got under way on Tuesday at the Pobitora Wildlife Sanctuary. Famous for its one-horned rhinoceros, the wildlife sanctuary is situated near Mayong in Morigaon district. The Wildlife Week will be celebrated with various events.
