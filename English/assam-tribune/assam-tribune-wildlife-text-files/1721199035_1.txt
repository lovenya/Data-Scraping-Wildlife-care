GUWAHATI, Oct 1 - The Regional Science Centre (RSC) Guwahati is going to celebrate �World Wild Life Week� from October 2 to 5 on its premises at Khanapara with a variety of participatory activities involving schoolchildren. Among the events planned for the period are exhibition on biological specimens and kits, painting competition, science quiz, popular science talk, feeding to pet birds and animals at Life Science corner by children, tree hunt contest, scientific film shows and live demonstrations on insects, fishes, pet birds and animals, stated a press release.
