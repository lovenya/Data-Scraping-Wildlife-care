Bajali, June 26: In a crackdown on wildlife crime, forest officials have apprehended two alleged poachers from the Manas National Park in Assam. The operation led to the seizure of a substantial quantity of illegal wildlife products and weapons. The accused, Kalicharan Basumatary and Maisrang Goyari, were allegedly involved in poaching activities within the park.
