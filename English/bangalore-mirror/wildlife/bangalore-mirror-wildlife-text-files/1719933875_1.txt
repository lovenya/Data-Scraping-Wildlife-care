Expressing deep concern over increasing man-animal conflicts in all forest reserves, Kumble stressed the need to conserve wildlife for the next generation. He said that he would visit all forest reserves in the state and submit a detailed report to the government on steps to protect and develop forests in the State and in creating awareness among the youth on conserving forests. “I have already visited Ranganathittu and Bandipur forest reserves in this regard,” he added. “The report will also draw attention to the menace of mining in the state that has threatened the forests,” he said.
