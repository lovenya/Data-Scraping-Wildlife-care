A youth has been arrested by the Forest Department after he performed a TikTok video holding a rabbit in Kabbalageri village in Badami taluk, Bagalkot district. 

The arrested youth has been identified as Arjun while his friend, Yellappa, who was also part of the act, is absconding. 

After they uploaded the video, the news reached the officials, who swung into action and arrested the youth under the Wildlife Protection Act. 

A lookout has been issued for Yellappa. 

Forest Department officials have warned people of arrest if anyone is found making videos with wild animals.
