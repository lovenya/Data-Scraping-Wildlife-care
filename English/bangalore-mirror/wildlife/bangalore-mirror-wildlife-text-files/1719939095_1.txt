People for Animals (PfA), Bengaluru received the Green NGO of the Year award at the 12th edition of the Olive Crown Awards 2022 held in Mumbai on Thursday. The annual award ceremony is organized by the International Advertising Association (IAA).
 
The awards acknowledged the remarkable work that the NGO has been doing by saving the Wildlife of Bengaluru PfA, Bengaluru has so far rescued over 31,200 urban wildlife of 210 different species of reptiles, mammals and a variety of birds and released them back into the wild.
