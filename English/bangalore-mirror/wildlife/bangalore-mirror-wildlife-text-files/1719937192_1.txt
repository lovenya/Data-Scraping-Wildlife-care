Chief Minister Siddaramaiah’s son Dr Yathindra has finally opened up about contesting from Varuna constitutency. Speaking to reporters on Tuesday, he said he was being pressured by the people of Varuna to make his debut in electoral politics. “I am working for the constituency. People are asking me to contest from their constituency. If the party provides me a ticket, I am ready to contest from Varuna. The decision is up to the party leaders. My father Siddaramiah has contributed immensely to Varuna constituency,” he said.
