Aman was reportedly killed in a suspected attack by a tiger on Monday at DB Kuppe wildlife range of Nagarahole National park of HD Kote Taluk in Mysuru district. The victim has been identified as Chinappa (36) and the incident happened at about 5.45 am when he stepped out to relieve himself. 

According to sources, on hearing his screams, people came out of their houses and rushed to help Chinnappa. His body was discovered after a long search by forest officials and villagers; he had suffered injuries on his neck and body. 

Chinappa is survived by wife, son and a daughter. Locals demanded a compensation of Rs 25 lakh and a job for his wife in the forest department. 

RFO Subrahamanya said that combing operation had begun.
