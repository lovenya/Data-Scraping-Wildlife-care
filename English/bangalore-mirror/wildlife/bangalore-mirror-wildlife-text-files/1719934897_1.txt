Leonardo DiCaprio reportedly raised a staggering $35 million at Christie's the 11th Hour charity event. The money raised through the auction will go to DiCaprio's environmental and wildlife conservation efforts. Thirty-three works by contemporary artists, including Ed Ruscha, Julian Schnabel, Banksy, Elizabeth Peyton, Takashi Murakami and Raymond Pettibon were on the block, with many fetching record prices. DiCaprio's The Great Gatsby co-star and close friend Tobey Maguire, Bradley Cooper, Mark Ruffalo and Salma Hayek were present at the auction.
