Forest officials have rescued a 15-year-old elephant, Mahalaxmi, which was illegally kept by an math on Hosur road. 

It is said that the owner was using it for processions and offering poojas in temples in different part of the state, violating the Wildlife Protection Act. Forest officials had seized Mahalaxmi three times earlier, but he managed to get it from their custody.

An official said, “this time, we will take stringent action against the owner as per the provisions of the Wildlife Protection Act.”
