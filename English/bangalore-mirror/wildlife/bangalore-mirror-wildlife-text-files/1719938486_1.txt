Kolkata: Expressing their grief, Prince William and Duchess of Cambridge Kate Middleton wrote a letter to the Director of Kaziranga National Park (KNP), P Sivakumar, about the large scale damage caused to the national park due to the floods in Assam this year.

"We have the happiest memories of our visit to Kaziranga in April 2016 and are shocked by what has happened. The deaths of so many animals, including one-horned rhino, is deeply upsetting. We know from our visit how dedicated all the staff in Kaziranga are in caring for the Park's wildlife, and can only begin to imagine what a difficult time this must be," the letter read.

He also appreciated the rescue work undertaken by the rangers and the park authority to rescue the animals during the devastating flood. They also expressed their concern over the loss of lives and livelihood caused due to the floods in addition to the COVID-19 pandemic.
