Acting on a tip-off, the sleuths, led by Ananthaiah, PSI, arrested Muniraju (35) and Devaraju (30), both hailing from Vemagal taluk of Kolar district.

According to senior officials of the forest cell, the duo were trying to sell 16 tortoises and four pond terrapins, which are protected under the Wildlife Act, 1972, for Rs 20,000, while the notional value of the amphibians was Rs 5 lakh, with each species costing Rs 25,000.

KR Puram police have registered a case under the Wildlife Protection Act.
