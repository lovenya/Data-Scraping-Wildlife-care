The carcass of the tiger was spotted by Wildlife guards at Nellikkapatti near the Mullaperiyar Dam in Idukki district yesterday, Wildlife sources said.

The tigress, which appeared to be a 10-year-old, seemed to have died of natural reasons as there were no outwoard sign to suspect it to be a victim of poaching, a Wildlife official said.
