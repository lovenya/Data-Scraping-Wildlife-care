The JD(S) legal cell on Saturday filed a complaint with Bengaluru City Police Commissioner T Suneel Kumar, seeking suitable action against a handful BJP leaders for trying to disturb law and order situation in the state.ADVERTISEMENTIn his complaint, Legal Cell president A P Ranganath has said that BJP leaders including B S Yeddyurappa were attacking Chief Minister H D Kumaraswamy using unparliamentary language. The BJP leaders were also trying to poach Congress and JD(S) MLAs with money and other allurements.As these developments could disrupt peace, the commissioner should take suitable legal action against them, the Cell has demanded.
