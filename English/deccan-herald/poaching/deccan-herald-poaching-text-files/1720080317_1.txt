The forest department officials recently arrested two persons who were engaged in poaching at the reserve forest.ADVERTISEMENTThe arrested persons are ex-serviceman Harishchandra of Karekoppa and Pramod of Talthareshettalli. They had illegally entered Yadavanadu Reserve Forest and were making preparations for poaching. A case has been registerd and both of them have been remanded in judicial custody.
