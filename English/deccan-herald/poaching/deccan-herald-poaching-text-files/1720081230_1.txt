Forest department personnel have arrested two persons, who had used country bombs to poach wild animals in the taluk.ADVERTISEMENTThe country bomb claimed the life of a jackal. Two accused, Raghavendra and Shivaramu, were arrested. Another accused, Gopi, is at large.According to sources, the accused had placed a country bomb in a farmland at Satyagala village in the taluk on Saturday night, which killed a jackal.The department personnel have seized two country bombs, a knife, mobile phone and the carcass of the jackal.
