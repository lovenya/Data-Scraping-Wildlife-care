Forest officials have arrested five people who were poaching wild animals by laying a trap at Nagarahole Tiger Reserve near Gonikoppa.

ADVERTISEMENTThe arrested are Kutta, Basava, Mutha, Kavala and Chubra, all residents of Paldalla Haadi. 

It is said that they were poaching animals by laying a trap near the periphery of the reserve forest. Assistant Conservator of Forest Satyanarayana said four tigers were killed after they laid the trap.
