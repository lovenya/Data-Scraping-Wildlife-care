A team led by range forest officer Devaraj arrested one person on charges of poaching wild animals by connecting electricity line to the solar fencing at Nagarahole National Park in the Anechowkur range Hunsur.

ADVERTISEMENTThe arrested has been identified as Shivanna, a resident of Chandanagiri Hadi on the fringes of Nagarahole National Park. The solar fencing on the fringes of the forest was not working for the past few days. Shivanna allegedly connected an electricity line from his house to solar fencing to hunt wild animals. On Saturday night, when he tried to repeat the practise, officials arrested Shivanna.
