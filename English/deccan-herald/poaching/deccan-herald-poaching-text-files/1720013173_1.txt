Dharani Kumar of Bhadravati, Umesh and Omkar of Channagiri taluk are the arrested. Kiran who hails from Chitradurga district has managed to escape. Police have recovered a leopard and a deer pelt from the arrested. 

Forest Department officials told Deccan Herald that the arrested were involved in selling the pelts of the animals poached in the region. Further information on poaching activities in forest bordering Bhadravati, Tarikere and Channagiri taluks is expected from the arrested.
