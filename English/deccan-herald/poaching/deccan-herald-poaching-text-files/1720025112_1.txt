The JD(S) had even kept three chartered flights ready to ferry the leaders. This plan was, however, foiled as the parties failed to obtain the required permissions to fly out.'The Congress leaders were led out of the Eagleton Resort in two buses by Vokkaliga strongman D K Shivakumar at around 11.30 pm. It is rumoured that the party is taking its MLA-elects to Hyderabad.Shortly after, the JD(S) leaders too left the Shangri La Hotel along with their party leader H D Kumaraswamy.Kumaraswamy's son Nikhil too was part of the entourage. It is rumoured that the JD(S) is taking its MLA-elects to Hyderabad.The parties have charged the BJP with tapping phones of all its leaders to track their movements.
