Discovery Channel’s new survival series Extreme Peril brings out some harrowing real life stories of people and their rescue. The series showcases what went wrong, what went right and how bravery and a little luck saved people lives.

Discover how a small town in Kansas is destroyed by a tornado. Watch how a pregnant skydiver recounts falling 10,000 feet and hitting the concrete when her two parachutes don’t open. 

Relive those terrifying moments as each testimonial deconstructs what happened, inside out. Watch this series on August 4  between 10 pm and 11 pm. Man vs Wild documents Bear Grylls effort to survive and find a way back to civilisation, usually requiring an overnight shelter of some kind. 

In each episode, Bear is left stranded in a region and finds ways to survive the ordeal.  In this episode, Ring Of Fire, Bear spends a week showing the viewers how to survive as well as navigate one’s way out of the region that was devastated by the 2004 tsunami. 

The region is a 400-km horseshoe shaped area that is prone to earthquakes and volcanic eruptions.  It has 452 volcanoes and hosts 90 per cent of the world’s earthquakes. Watch this thrilling episode on Discovery Channel on August 3 between 9 pm and 10 pm.

Animal Planet presents Yellowstone on August 3 at 10 pm which takes a look at a year in the life of Yellowstone National Park.

It examines how its wildlife adapts to living in one of the harshest wilderness on  Earth. Autumn is Yellowstone’s shortest season and a period of swift change.

Conditions change from summer to winter in just two months, forcing animals to leave or prepare for winter. From Bison to Bull Elk, discover how these animals adapt to change.
