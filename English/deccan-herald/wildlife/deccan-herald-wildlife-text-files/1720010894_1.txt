He was speaking to presspersons after visiting Girijana Shikshana Samsthe Director M V Prabhu who is getting treatment at the jail ward of district hospital. Prabhu was arrested on the charges of illegally entering Nagarahole Reserve Forest. 

He said “crores of rupees are being released for the welfare of tribals and protection of wildlife. However, we are hearing lot of corruption taking place in the implementation. I will also verify the use of funds released by the Centre and send a report to the Government.” 

Wildlife act is in force for the protection of wildlife and National Park. However, filing a complaint against an voluntary organisation officer who has concern towards tribals is violation.  The MP assured to provide justice to Prabhu.
