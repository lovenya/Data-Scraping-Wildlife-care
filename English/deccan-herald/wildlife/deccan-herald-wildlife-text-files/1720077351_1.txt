The awards for the 66th annual Filmfare awards were announced on Saturday, with Taapsee Pannu and Saif Ali Khan among the big winners at the event.ADVERTISEMENTAmong the winners was the late Irrfan Khan, who won the award for best actor in a leading role (male) category, while Amitabh Bachchan bagged the best actor (critics) Award. Alaya F took home the best debut (Female) award for her role in Jawaani Jaaneman while Taapsee Pannu and Tilottama Shome won the award for best actress (popular) and award for best actress (Critics) respectively for their roles in Thappad and Sir.Saif Ali Khan and Om Raut also won big, with Khan taking home the award for best actor in a supporting role (male) and Raut being honoured with the award for best director for Tanhaji.
