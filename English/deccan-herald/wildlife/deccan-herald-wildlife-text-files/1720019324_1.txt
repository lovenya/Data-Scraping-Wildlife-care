“After giving them a rest for seven days, the animals are now transferred to the cage for public view,” said Bihar Deputy Chief Minister Sushil Kumar Modi. This is for the first time in the last four decades that the park is getting Asiatic lions. Earlier, it had two hybrid lioness, but according to the established norms of the Central Zoo Authority, breeding of hybrid lions is prohibited in zoos. 

“We will give Hyderabad zoo a pair of Indian single-horned rhinoceros,” said zoo director Abhay Kumar.

Approval awaited
The zoo authorities are awaiting approval for a proposal sent to the Central Zoo Authority to further enrich the wildlife family. 

“One such proposal is that the  zoo will give a female rhino and a pair of hippopotamus to the Sipahijala zoo in Tripura in lieu of a pair of spectacled languor, a pair of clouded leopard, a pair of leopard cat and a silver and a golden pheasant,” said a zoo official, and added that once the plan is approved, a few more animals could be seen.
