Two Madikeri natives were arrested in Bengaluru on Monday trying to sell tusks, according to a forest department officer. ADVERTISEMENTThe forest department apprehended the men, aged 21 and 20, in Annapoorneshwari Nagar, West Bengaluru, following a tip-off, a senior officer said. The suspects, who have no criminal record, have been booked under the Wildlife Act, the officer added.
