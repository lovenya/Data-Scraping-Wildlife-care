Chamarajnagar: The bodies of an adult tiger and a cub were found in a decomposed state in an empty land near the border of Melur-Terakanambi villages, Gundlupet and Chamarajanagar taluk in Chamarajanagar district on Thursday. ADVERTISEMENTThe spot where the bodies were found is 21 km away from the border of Biligiri Ranganatha Swamy Temple Tiger Reserve and 200 meters from the border of Terakanambi village of Gundlupet taluk. Chamarajnagar Wildlife Division Assistant Conservator of Forest (ACF), Chamarajnagar regional range forest officer and other forest department personnel visited the spot. An expert team including Dr Manjunath, veterinarian of Bengaluru Bannerghatta Biological Park, has taken measures to conduct the post-mortem as per NTCA guidelines. Officers said that the cause of the death would be known only after post-mortem.
