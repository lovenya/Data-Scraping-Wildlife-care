Bhadra Wildlife Division Deputy Conservator of Forest Vijay Mohan Raj has said that the population of tigers in Bhadra Reserve Forest is satisfactory and they are not facing any threat. He said that a tigress had given birth to 2 cubs at Hulikere-Thadabehalla recently and the tourists have spotted the tiger with its cubs. 

Another tigress had given birth to two cubs at Aldar area.ADVERTISEMENT
