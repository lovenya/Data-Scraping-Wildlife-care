A tiger enclosure and a two-lakh-litre capacity overhead tank were inaugurated at the Bannerghatta Biological Park on Friday.

ADVERTISEMENTInfosys Foundation chairperson Sudha N Murty, Zoo Authority of Karnataka chairperson Rehana Banu, principal chief conservator of forests (wildlife) Ravi Ralp, Zoo Authority of Karnataka secretary Abhiyu Singh, Mysore Zoo executive director Venkateshan and BBP executive director Santosh Kumar inaugurated the enclosure and the tank.

The enclosure has been constructed at a cost of Rs 1.25 crore and the overhead tank at Rs 65 lakh. Infosys Foundation funded the project.

During her last visit to the park, Sudha Murty had named two tiger cubs - Krishna and Anushka, which were released into the new enclosure, along with their mother Vishmaya, on Friday.
