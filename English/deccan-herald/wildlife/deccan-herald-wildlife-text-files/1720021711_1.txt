The expo would comprise display of stamps carrying the pictures of waterfowls, storks, sea birds, penguin, kingfisher, heron, song birds, woodpecker, cuckoos, orile, bustard, waterbirds, birds of prey, owls, hornbill, pelican, and terns and so on. 

As many as 1,000 stamps exclusively on ducks will be exhibited on the occasion. Daniel has organised as many as 159 exhibitions on philately till date. He has received as many as 21 national level and 10 international level awards from various institutions.ADVERTISEMENT
