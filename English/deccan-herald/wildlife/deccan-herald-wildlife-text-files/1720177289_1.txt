A 55-year-old woman was killed after a leopard attacked her in a village near Katarniyaghat wildlife sanctuary here, police said.ADVERTISEMENTPhoolkesari had come out of her house for some work yesterday in Badlupurva Karikot village near the Nishanghada range of the sanctuary when the leopard, hiding nearby, attacked her, they said.Even as she raised an alarm, the leopard dragged her in the forest and before the locals could arrive she died, they said, adding that the body was sent for post-mortem examination.
