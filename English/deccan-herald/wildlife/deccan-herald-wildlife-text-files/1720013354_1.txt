Swearing revenge

HBO presents Collateral Damage starring Arnold Schwarzenegger, John Leguizamo, John Turturro and Francesca Neri on May 7 at 9 pm. Firefighter Gordon Brewer is done putting out fires and ready to start one of his own. When his wife and child are horrifically killed in an international terrorist bombing, linked to Claudio The Wolf Perrini — the CIA and FBI offer no answers and no response. 

With the official investigation going nowhere, and haunted by the thought of his family’s killer walking away scot-free, Gordon swears revenge even if it takes him to Colombia. 

Jaw-dropping majesty
 
Join Nigel Marvin in the Canadian Arctic for the most spectacular exposure of the Arctic. Nigel follows the wildlife over a nine month period while exploring their unique environs. He takes a dogsled ride from Churchill to see foxes and caribou. He also witnesses the jaw-dropping majesty of the Northern Lights in minus 30 degree weather. Watch this magnificent programme as Nigel’s affinity with animals helps him truly discover their characteristics and personalities. Arctic Exposure With Nigel Marvin airs on May 7 at 9 pm on Animal Planet.
