A leopard cub was killed by a speeding vehicle in Chhattisgarh’s Kanker district on Saturday, a forest official said. The animal's body was found along the Kanker-Korar road near village Bardeori, said forest ranger Sandeep Singh.ADVERTISEMENTCiting autopsy findings, the official said that the 5-month-old cub was hit by a speeding vehicle.
