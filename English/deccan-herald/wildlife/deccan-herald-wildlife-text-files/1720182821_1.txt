Beckoning children this summer, the Bannerghatta Biological Park is holding summer camps.ADVERTISEMENTStarting from April 24 to May 3rd, the park will conduct camps for children of a group of 6 to 10 years every day with 30 children in each batch.Every day the camps will be held from 9:30 am to 5:00 pm at the park where children will be taught about various aspects of zoo management, animal behaviour, careers in zoo and insight into wildlife conservation.The kids will be taken around the zoo, butterfly park and safaris and provided with lunch and snacks.For registration and further details, contact educationbbp@gmail.com or call 8884414262 or 080- 29776466.
