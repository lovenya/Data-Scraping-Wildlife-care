The Health department will observe World Zoonoses Day on July 6, with review meetings across multiple departments and awareness campaigns among the public.ADVERTISEMENTAt the district/municipality level, the department will hold meetings with veterinary, wildlife, environment and forest departments to discuss priority zoonotic diseases and develop joint action plans. According to a press release from the department, they will also communicate with the local veterinary departments and clinics about organising vaccination drives for animals. Awareness campaigns will be held among pet owners and animal handlers, along with contests and talks for students.
