A man has been arrested for keeping three orange spider conches, a rare and endangered species of sea snail, in an aquarium at a restaurant near India Gate, the police said on Tuesday.

ADVERTISEMENTNitin Bajaj, a resident of Lajpat Nagar, was arrested on January 9 from ‘Have More Restaurant’ at 11-12, Pandara Road for violating the Wildlife Protection Act.

According to the Wildlife Protection Act, orange spider conches, found in the Indian Ocean, are placed in the Schedule IV and keeping, buying or selling them is illegal “The action was taken over a complaint by the Maneka Gandhi-led People For Animals,” police said.
