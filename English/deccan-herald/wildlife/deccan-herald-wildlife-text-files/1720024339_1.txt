The forest officials doused the forest fire, which was noticed in Someshwar reserve forest range, two days ago. ADVERTISEMENT According to eye witness the fire was noticed on Saturday itself. The forest fire has damaged flora and fauna in 15 acre land at Ultha reserve forest. 

When the officials realised that the fire is spreading to Hanja, Karimane, Edamale, they rushed to the spot and doused the fire. 

Someshwar Reserve Forest wildlife division official Lohith Kumar said one acre forest in Hanja region has been engulfed with forest fire on Sunday. The officials doused the fire at late night.

The issue of forest fire has been brought to the notice of senior officials. “We have already installed board to preserve forest from fire on either side of highway situated adjacent to the reserve forest,” he added.
