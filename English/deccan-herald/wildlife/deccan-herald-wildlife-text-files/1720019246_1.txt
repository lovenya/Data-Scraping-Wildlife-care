The 8,800-km long journey is organised by Rajasthan Sangh Karnataka (RSK) with the objective of spreading awareness on various issues related to young and rural children across the country. The group is expected to restart from Davangere on Saturday morning. As per plan, they will reach Jammu on the 15th day and will head for Kargil by July 18. 
The bikers, Darshit, Govind, Praveen, Pranav, Chetan and Nitesh are part of Prakruti, an NGO and volunteered to impart knowledge to children in cities like Pune, Mumbai, Jaipur, Delhi, Ladakh and more. 

Importance of education, ill effects of drugs, wildlife issues and importance of public transport are some key issues the six men group will look at discussing at schools and colleges.ADVERTISEMENT
