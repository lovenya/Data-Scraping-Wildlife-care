The energy and enthusiasm of children brought wave of joy in the Nisargadhama and even tourists enjoyed the programme along with students. Drawing, essay and quiz competitions were organised for school students as part of wild life week. Students seated on grass under shade of trees, poured their imaginations on paper on the theme was ‘wildlife’. Programme was inaugurated by CCF Nagaraj. He said that it is the responsibility of human beings to take care of nature.  DCF Karunakar, ACF M Shashidhar, Vijay Kumar, RFO M S Chinnappa and others were present.ADVERTISEMENT
