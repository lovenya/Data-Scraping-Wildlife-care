Of the rescued snakes, only the cobra and viper are venomous snakes and the rest were non-venomous.”Another volunteer Jayaraj said, “Along with the snakes, we also rescued two terrapins.”Amid the panic, several people were catching terrapins to rear them as pets. Chirantan, a resident of Prakruthi Enclave, said, “People caught turtles believing they would bring good fortune according to vaastu shastra.”
