Haunted by a strange presence

Catch ‘Uninhabited’ on Zee Studio at 4.55 pm on January 8. The film is about a young couple, Harry and Beth, who decide to go to a remote and deserted coral island for a camping holiday.ADVERTISEMENT The island is idyllic, surrounded by a wide reef, covered with palms and full of birds and other wildlife. While having fun on the island, the young lovers soon come to believe that there is someone else on the island. Things go missing from their camp and then they discover someone else’s footprints in the sand. At first, they’re amused — but the couple soon find that the island is inhabited by a ghost, seeking retribution for a past outrage.
