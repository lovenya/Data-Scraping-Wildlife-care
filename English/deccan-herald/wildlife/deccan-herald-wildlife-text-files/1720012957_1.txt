What makes the place more special is that it is Rahul Dravid’s favourite travel destination in India.

Dravid needs no introduction, does he? He is renowned world-over for his impeccable cricketing abilities. Ask this lad from Karnataka, why he loves Kabini and he replies, “The place exudes the feeling of an Indian forest.” Situated on the banks of the river Kabini, it was a popular hunting spot for British Viceroys and Indian royalty. 

Travel tips

* How to get there: The wildlife sanctuary is accessible by rail, road and air. It is about 220 kms from Bangalore. Buses and trains are available from Mysore. 

* Best time to visit: Visit Kabini anytime between October and May. Avoid visiting between June and September as the region experiences heavy rainfall.   

* Accommodation: Options available are Orange County Resort, Cicada Kabini, Kabini River Lodge, among others.ADVERTISEMENT
