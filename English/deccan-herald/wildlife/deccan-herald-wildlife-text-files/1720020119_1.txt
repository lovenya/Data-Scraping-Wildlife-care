Watch Austin Stevens, Jeff Corwin, Nick Baker, Saba Douglas, Jane Goodall and Nigel Marven as they live every moment of their lives as crusaders of these terrifying but terrific creatures. 

Being bitten by a viper and wrangling worms is not something that puts them off. They defy danger and death to become the true ‘Masters Of The Jungle’.


Finding a way back

In each episode, Grylls is left stranded in a region and uses his efforts to survive and find a way back to civilisation. Along the way, he braves the biting cold, encounters dangerous predators and for food, he has to make do with whatever he can catch or find — dead or alive. ‘Man Vs Wild’ airs on August 30 at 9 pm on Discovery Channel.
