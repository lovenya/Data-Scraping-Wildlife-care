He will be followed by other renowned photographers that Nikon has collaborated to encourage people to learn new skills, as they are staying safe at their homes amid the lockdown across the country. Nikon India will offer classes in various popular themes including Wedding, Wildlife, Effects of Focal Length, Interior & Architecture, Street, Portrait, Food & Pet Photography and more.The company promises that the tutorials will be taught by professional photographers to offer in-depth photography knowledge to help users capture stunning photos and videos.
