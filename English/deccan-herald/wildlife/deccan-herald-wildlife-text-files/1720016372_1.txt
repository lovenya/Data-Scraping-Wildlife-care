A proposal has been submitted to State Wildlife division Chief Conservator of Forest to capture two rogue elephants at Siddapura and Palibetta 15 days ago, said Kodagu Circle Coservator of Forest K B Markandeya. Speaking to reporters, he said rogue elephants have been causing damage to the farmers and growers. “We have already got the photographs of these rogue elephants. Once we get the permission, two rogue elephants will be captured,” he added.ADVERTISEMENT
