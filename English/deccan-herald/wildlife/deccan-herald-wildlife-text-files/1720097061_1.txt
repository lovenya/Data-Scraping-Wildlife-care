A wild jumbo has given birth to twin calves in the Bandipur Tiger Reserve forest area recently. This is said to be a rare incident and has not been observed in the Bandipur region so far.ADVERTISEMENTThe wild jumbo, along with its calves, was photographed by the wild enthusiasts on a safari on April 18. It is suspected that the jumbo might have given birth to the calves on April 17.
