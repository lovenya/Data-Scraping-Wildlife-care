The Hindu Spiritual and Service Foundation has organised a Hindu Spiritual and Service Fair–2016 from December 14 to 18 at the National High School grounds here. 

ADVERTISEMENTOver 250 Hindu socio-religious service institutions from across Karnataka are expected to showcase their service activities by setting up stalls at the five-day conclave. The chairman of the reception committee of the fair, Kasturirangan, told reporters here that the six themes to be covered during the fair include: Conserve forest and protect wildlife; sustain environment; foster women’s honour; preserve ecology; inculcate family and human values and instil patriotism.  

The fair is scheduled to be inaugurated by spiritual leader Sri Sri Ravishankar at 5pm on December 14, he added.
