The two are being treated at Manipal hospital .

The tiger was first spotted by a motorcyclist who was heading towards Shikaripura from Eesuru. By 6 in the morning, most villagers saw the tiger roaming on the outskirts of the village. As the news spread, villagers came in groups to see the tiger.

As police struggled to control the crowd, Forest department and Wildlife division staff arrived at the spot. Efforts to capture the tiger that began 7 am ended successfully after the beast was tranquilised and captured at 6 in the evening.ADVERTISEMENT
