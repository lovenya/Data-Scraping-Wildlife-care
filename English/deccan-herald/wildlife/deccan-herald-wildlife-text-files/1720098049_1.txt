As many as five villagers sustained injuries in a wolf attack at Holesirigere village near Malebennur in Harihar taluk on Tuesday.ADVERTISEMENTThe wolf entered the farm houses in the village and attacked the villagers.The injured were taken to the General Hospital in Harihar after being given first aid at the Primary Health Centre.It was reported that stray dogs attacked the wolf and it succumbed to the injuries.
