Kuduremukh Wildlife division and Wetsern Ghat Task Force will organise a programme on wildlife protection and welfare of tribals on June 22 at Anantha Padmanabha hall in Hebri. The meet will provide information on facilities given to the tribals by the government. It will also discuss on the destruction of food crops by wild animals. The meet will be inaugurated by Western Ghat Task Force Chairman Anantha Hegde Asheesara.ADVERTISEMENT
