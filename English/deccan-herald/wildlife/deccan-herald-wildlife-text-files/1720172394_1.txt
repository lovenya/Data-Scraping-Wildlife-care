The state government on Monday transferred 10 IFS officers with immediate effect. 

Prominent among the officers who have been transferred and their new postings are as follows: K M Narayanaswamy, Conservator of Forest, Working Plan, Chikkamagaluru; K Kantharaju, Conservator of Forests and Director, Bhadra Wildlife, Chikkamagaluru; Prakash S Netalkar, Conservator of Forests and MD, Karnataka Compost Development Corporation, Bengaluru; Rathan U Kelkar, Director AYUSH, Bengaluru.
