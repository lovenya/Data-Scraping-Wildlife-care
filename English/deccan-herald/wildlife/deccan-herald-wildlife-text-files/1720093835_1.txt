A video clip of an encounter between a black (Melanistic) leopard and a leopard, atop a tree, said to be at Nagarahole Tiger Reserve, has gone viral on social media.ADVERTISEMENTAccording to the sources, the video was captured on March 6 by a wildlife enthusiast, during a safari. The video captured for nearly two minutes shows the black leopard climbing a tall tree where a leopard is seen crouching. The black leopard tries to charge on the leopard. However, after a while, the black leopard climbs down the tree.According to wildlife enthusiasts, both are male and it might have been a possible territory fight.
