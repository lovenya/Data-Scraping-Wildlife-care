ADVERTISEMENTWildest Africa’ is a celebration of Africa’s most spectacular locations, showcasing the full African experience. The four-part series journeys from the cradle of human civilisation to the most awe-inspiring natural wonders of the world by going on a safari to see the continent’s Big Five – Lion, Elephant, Leopard, Black and White Rhino and Cape Buffalo. 

‘Wildest Africa’ discovers their secret locations, their culture and wildlife issues, as well as how the natural wonders they live in are being threatened and what is being done to overcome these environmental pressures. 

Tune in to this episode on May 17 at 8 pm on Animal Planet.

Every air crash is a catastrophe that leaves behind families and ruined lives. 

But each air crash is also a lesson learnt. After each disaster one knows how to build safer planes, evacuate passengers more swiftly and avoid collisions. 

Using powerful personal accounts from survivors, pilots and witnesses and aviation experts, this episode of ‘Air Crash Confidential’ on Discovery Channel, May 17 at 8 pm, investigates a selection of aviation disasters to reveal how and why tragedy still happens.
