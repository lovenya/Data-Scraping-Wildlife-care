A bonanza of entertainment 
WB presents two back-to-back superhit films on October 22. Watch Sphere starring Dustin Hoffman, Sharon Stone, Samuel L Jackson and Peter Coyote at 9 pm. When a team of scientists — psychologist Norman Goodman, biochemist Beth Halperin and mathematician Harry Adams — descend into the depths of murky ocean to explore an ancient spacecraft resting on the Pacific floor, they discover the ship's mysterious cargo.

 Contact with a murderous alien intelligence proves deadly. With all surface communications cut off and casualties rising, the desperate survivors must face a shocking truth.

The Perfect Storm starring George Clooney, Mark Wahlberg and Diane Lane airs at 11.40 pm. Just days after returning from a disappointing fishing trip, Andrea Gail captain Billy Tyne decides to return to the sea to trawl for swordfish one last time before the season ends. 

From the port of Gloucester, Massachusetts, Tyne and a five-man crew that includes seaman Bobby Shatford set sail for the Flemish Cap, rich fishing grounds far beyond the vessel’s normal range. Out at sea, Tyne learns of an impending storm but thinks he can outrun it.

TLC presents Shimmy on October 22 at 9.30 pm. For thousands of years women from the cradle of civilisation have maintained their beauty and explored their sensuality with the ancient practice of belly dance.

The new series Shimmy, designed by Kim Pechet, a belly dance instructor and fitness professional, brings the secrets of this exotic practice to women all over the world.
Join Kim as she brings a relaxing warm-up in calming, lush and warm surroundings in preparation for the more vigorous movements. 

Watch brilliant dance movements and energetic, low impact aerobic choreography designed to burn the calories.
