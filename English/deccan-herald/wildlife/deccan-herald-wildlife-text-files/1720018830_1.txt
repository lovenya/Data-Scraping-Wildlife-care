ADVERTISEMENTMagnitude Gallery is bringing you ‘Junglee — Magnificently Wild’, a collection of paintings that depict majestic wild animals in their natural habitat this month between 10 am and 8 pm. 

The gallery is located at 140/13, 27th Cross, 13th Main, 3rd Block, Jayanagar. 

Festival time

This is the second edition of the festival and will see participation from artists and galleries across India. it will be held till June 25. 

Monsoon sale

Crimson-The Art Resource on Cunningham Road, is having its 18th annual monsoon sale of paintings till June 30. 

‘Basuki’ 

Kynkyny Art, in association with J M Financial is presenting Basuki 2011, featuring latest mixed media works of Basuki Dasgupta, at Kynkyny Art  till July 2. 

‘Imprints’

Nature Lounge is hosting a photo exhibition ‘Imprints’ in India (Bangalore) where photographers will be invited to showcase their work, under the theme of ‘Nature and wildlife’, from July 1 to July 5 at Chitrakala Parishat, 10.30 am to 7 pm. ‘Imprints’ is the portrayal of nature in its raw form. 

Group show

Renaissance Gallerie is holding a group art show till June 17 at its premises. On exhibition will be paintings by Lo­keswara Rao, Eknath Giram, Anand Mali and Dnyati Wagh.
