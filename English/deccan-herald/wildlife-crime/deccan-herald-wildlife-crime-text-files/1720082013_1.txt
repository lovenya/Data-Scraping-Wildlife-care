A farmer found 2 leopard cubs on July 14 in the Kotla village of Haryana’s Nuh district. ADVERTISEMENTThe cubs were safely handed over to the Wildlife Department. “Some children had gone to the forest to let goats graze, and they found these cubs. We will take them to the forest and try to reach their mother,” said Rajesh Kumar, Divisional Forest Officer of Haryana.
