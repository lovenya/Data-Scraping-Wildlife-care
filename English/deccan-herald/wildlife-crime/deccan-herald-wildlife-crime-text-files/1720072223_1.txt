Fifty-six persons were arrested after a clash which broke out on March 31 in Jalgaon, Maharashtra over playing music outside a Mosque while Namaz was going on, informed Krushikesh Rawale, ASP, Jalgaon.  While speaking to ANI, he said, “The situation is peaceful now and till now we have arrested 56 people. The accused are being identified with the help of CCTV footage. Section 144 of CrPC has been implemented till tomorrow.”ADVERTISEMENT
