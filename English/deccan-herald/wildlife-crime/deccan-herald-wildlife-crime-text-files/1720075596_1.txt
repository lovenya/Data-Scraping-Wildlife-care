A 19-year-old girl filed a complaint alleging gang-rape against two in Badagaon of Murar Police Station limits on June 17. Police registered a case under IPC Section 376. While talking to ANI, Murar CSP Vinayak Shukla said, “A 19-year-old complainant has filed a case alleging gang-rape against two accused in Badagaon of Murar Police Station limits. The investigation is under way. Case registered under section 376 of IPC. Action will be taken on the basis of the investigation.”ADVERTISEMENT
