A local Bharatiya Janata Party leader Jitu Chaudhary was shot dead in Delhi's Mayur Vihar phase-3 on April 20. A few empty cartridges and other important evidence have been recovered from the crime scene.ADVERTISEMENTThe accused are absconding, and the search for eyewitnesses and CCTV footage is underway. The victim had gunshot injuries and was declared ‘brought dead’ at the hospital. Further details are awaited.
