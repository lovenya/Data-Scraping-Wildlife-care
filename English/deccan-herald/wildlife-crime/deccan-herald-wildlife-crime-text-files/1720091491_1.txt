The Supreme Court on Tuesday said that any act of sexual assault and harassment against children should be viewed very seriously and all such offences have to be dealt with in a stringent manner as it is a crime against humanity and society.ADVERTISEMENT"Children are precious human resources of our country; they are the country’s future. The hope of tomorrow rests on them. But unfortunately, in our country, a girl child is in a very vulnerable position," a bench of Justices M R Shah and B V Nagarathna said."There are different modes of her exploitation.
