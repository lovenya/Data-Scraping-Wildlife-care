Madhya Pradesh Police arrested five people with demonetised currency notes in Indore. The demonetised currency notes are worth Rs 3.76 lakh. According to Indore Crime Branch Additional SP G Parashar, “5 people were arrested with demonetised notes with a face value of Rs 3.76 lakhs in Indore. The main accused is said to be a practitioner of black magic and had promised other accused to turn the notes into new currency. A complaint is registered and further investigation is underway.”ADVERTISEMENT
