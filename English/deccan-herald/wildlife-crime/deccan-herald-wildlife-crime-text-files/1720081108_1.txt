Prime Minister Narendra Modi arrived at Bandipur Tiger Reserve on Sunday and went on a wildlife safari. ADVERTISEMENTPM Modi reached Mysuru late Saturday to take part in the programme to mark 50 years of Project Tiger here on Sunday. He left Mysuru at 7 am by helicopter and reached Bandipur at 7.20 am at a helipad near Melukamanahalli. He will return to Mysuru for the Project Tiger programme and will also release 'Tiger Estimation Report of 2022' at the KSOU convocation hall. He will also visit Mudumalai Tiger Reserve in Tamil Nadu and interact with Bomman-Bellie couple of the Oscar-winning documentary Elephant Whisperers.
