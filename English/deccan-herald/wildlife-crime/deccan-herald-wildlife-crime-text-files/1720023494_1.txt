The CM should keep his word or face the consequences,” he said.Stating that he had no faith in the NIA (National Investigation Agency) investigation into the murder, Muthalik said all those arrested should be booked under the Karnataka Control of Organised Crimes Act (KCOCA).“There is a need to end Islamic jihadi mentality,” he said, adding that Sri Rama Sene would ban the Wakf board campaign in the future.
