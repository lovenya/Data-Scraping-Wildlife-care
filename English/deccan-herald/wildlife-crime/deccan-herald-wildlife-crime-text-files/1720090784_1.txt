The Forest department personnel arrested three people, including a person from Tamil Nadu, on charges of illegal entry into Cauvery Wildlife Sanctuary and planning to hunt wild animals, on Wednesday night.ADVERTISEMENTThe arrested are - Aiyyan, Shivakumar of Athuru village near Gopinatham and Marimuthu from Tamil Nadu.Forest guard Chandrashekar and an employee of Gundlupet anti-poaching squad, on patrol, found the trio moving at the Metugallu forest area and alerted the higher officials.A team, led by Range Forest Officer Nischit, rushed to the spot and arrested them. Two country rifles, one two-wheeler, battery, two knives and bullets were recovered from them. The accused confessed to poaching a deer earlier. They have been remanded to judicial custody.
