Meet a 72-year-old Antarjyami Sahoo from Nayagarh, a retired teacher who is an environment enthusiast. He has planted more than 30 thousand trees since the early 60s. “I have been planting trees since 1961. And even today, I work towards saving the environment. It makes me happy. Government must plant more trees to give food and shelter to the wildlife,” said Antarjyami Sahoo.ADVERTISEMENT
