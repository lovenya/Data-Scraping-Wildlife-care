Four suspected narcotics smugglers were arrested with smack worth over Rs 1 crore, police in this Uttar Pradesh district said on Friday.ADVERTISEMENTJibrail, Mobin, Sufian and Zubair were arrested from the Ramgaon area along the Bahraich-Nepal Road on Thursday, Superintendent of Police Prashant Verma said.The police also seized 350 grams of smack worth over Rs 1 crore in the international market from them, Verma added.The quartet has been booked under the Narcotics Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act and a probe is under way, the police said.
