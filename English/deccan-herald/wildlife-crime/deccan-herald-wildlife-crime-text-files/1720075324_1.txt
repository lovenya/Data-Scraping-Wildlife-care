Ballia: A constable of the Railway Protection Force was suspended for allegedly molesting and assaulting a female Railway employee at the Suraimanpur Railway Station here.ADVERTISEMENTDinesh Singh allegedly molested the woman on Wednesday evening when she was leaving for home.
