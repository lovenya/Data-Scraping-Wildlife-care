Ashish Mishra, son of MoS Home Affairs Ajay Mishra Teni arrived at the Crime Branch office, Lakhimpur on October 9 in connection with the Lakhimpur Kheri violence that took place on October 3 in which eight persons including four farmers were killed. He was earlier summoned by the Uttar Pradesh Police in connection with the probe in the matter. Earlier, on Friday, Ajay Mishra Teni had said that his son was unable to report to police due to health reasons.ADVERTISEMENT
