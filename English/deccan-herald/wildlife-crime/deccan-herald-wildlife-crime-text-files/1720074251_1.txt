The Malleshwaram police have apprehended a 50-year-old bicycle thief and confiscated 19 bicycles with a combined value of Rs 5 lakh.ADVERTISEMENTThe accused Shekar M, resides in Dayanandanagar. The accused used to target homes in Malleswaram, Vyalikaval, Yeshwantpur, Sheshadripuram, where bicycles were parked. He focused on expensive bicycles.
