Gadchiroli: In a major intelligence-based operation, police and security forces seized explosives and claymore mines from the Tipagad area of Gadchiroli district of Maharashtra, which are used to carry out IED attacks. ADVERTISEMENTGadchiroli district’s Superintendent of Police Neelotpal confirmed the development. After getting the information, two teams of Bomb Detection and Disposal Squad (BDDS),  one unit of C-60 commando team and Quick Action Team (QAT) of Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) were dispatched. <img src="https://images.deccanherald.com/deccanherald%2F2024-05%2F9565c1c3-5e93-4eab-abbe-f873e669c164%2FIMG_20240506_WA0056.jpg?w=1200" alt="Six pressure cookers filled with explosives and detonators were seized. Credit: By Special Arrangement“When the teams reached the spot, they found 6 pressure cookers filled with explosives and detonators and 3 claymore pipes filled with explosives and shrapnels were also found. Remaining 3 claymore pipes were without any explosive. Teams also found gunpowder in a plastic bag, medicines and blankets at the same spot,” Neelotpal said.“A total 9 IEDs and 3 claymore pipes were destroyed in situ by the BDDS team. Remaining materials have been burnt on the spot,” he added.
