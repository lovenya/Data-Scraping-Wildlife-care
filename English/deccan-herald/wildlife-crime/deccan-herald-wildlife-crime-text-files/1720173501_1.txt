Police have arrested two persons in the firing incident involving AIMIM Chief Asaduddin Owaisi on February 3, Uttar Pradesh Additional Director General of Law and Order Prashant Kumar said. The arrested persons will be produced before the court. “Police arrested two persons for firing on the car of AIMIM MP Asaduddin Owaisi yesterday. The arms used in the crime were recovered. The accused said that they were hurt by his remarks against a particular religion. They will be produced before the court,” Prashant Kumar said.ADVERTISEMENT
