Bengaluru: Authorities on Wednesday discovered a note in one of the washrooms of the Kempegowda International Airport (KIA), warning of bombs being planted at the airport. ADVERTISEMENT"Bomb in A3 and T1, will blast in 25 mins," the note read, according to a senior police officer. Bomb and canine squads searched the airport premises and declared the threat a hoax. Police are yet to file an FIR.
