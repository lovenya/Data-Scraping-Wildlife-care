After a minor Dalit boy was allegedly beaten to death by a teacher in Surana village of Rajasthan, Congress leader Sachin Pilot said that Faith must be instilled in people, especially in minds of Dalit community that crime against them won’t go unnoticed and they will receive justice. “The innocent student died after being beaten by teacher in school. FIR says that he was trying to drink water from teacher's pot of water. Family said he was buried during night. Lathi charge done on family. Faith must be instilled in people, especially in minds of Dalit community that no one will be spared after committing a crime against them,” he said on August 16. ADVERTISEMENT
