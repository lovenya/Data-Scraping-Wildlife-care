Senior Notary and Taluk Panchayat former president M B Abhimanyu Kumar has alleged that his Facebook account has been hacked by miscreants.ADVERTISEMENTIn a complaint submitted to the cyber crime police in Madikeri, he said that through the hacked account, the miscreants have placed a demand for money from his friends.Some of them have fallen prey and have transferred the money as well, he said.
