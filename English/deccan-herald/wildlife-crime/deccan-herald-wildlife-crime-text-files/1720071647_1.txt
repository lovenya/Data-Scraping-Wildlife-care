New Delhi: The Delhi Police on Friday arrested a man for allegedly abusing and disrespecting Hindu gods and goddesses by selling their obscene images online.ADVERTISEMENTA case under section 295 of the Indian Penal Code and 67A of the Information Technology Act was registered by the IFSO unit of Delhi Police's Special Cell after it got a complaint from the Delhi Commission for Women last week.The DCW issued the notice after it received a complaint regarding the objectionable pictures being sold on the internet.An official said one person is arrested and a few more are yet to be apprehended.
