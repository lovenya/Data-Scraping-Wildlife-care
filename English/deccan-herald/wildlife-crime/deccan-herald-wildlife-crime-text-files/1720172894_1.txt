Hyderabad: A 14-year-old Dalit boy was allegedly tied up and beaten by a man on the city outskirts for plucking a pomegranate from his house, police said on Wednesday.ADVERTISEMENTThe incident took place on June 22 at Kesaram village of Shabad mandal.
