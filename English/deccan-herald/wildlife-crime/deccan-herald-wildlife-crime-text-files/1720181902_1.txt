The Anti-Human Trafficking Unit of the Delhi Police busted a prostitution racket that was functional in Delhi. Delhi Police DCP Crime Vichitra Veer on July 23 informed that the police arrested 5 persons in connection with the case. He further informed that the police also rescued 10 women. 
“Anti-Human Trafficking Unit of Delhi police has busted a prostitution racket. One of the accused sent the girls to India on the pretext of getting them a job but later pushed them into sex rackets. Out of 5 accused arrested, 3 are foreign nationals. Further probe on,” the DCP Crime said.
