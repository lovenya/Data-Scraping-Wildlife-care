A bomb blast incident took place on February 05 near the Margram hospital junction area of Birbhum, West Bengal. A TMC worker has died and two people have been injured in the bomb blast. The name of the deceased is Newton Sheikh who is the brother of TMC leader Lalu Sheikh. Lalu Sheikh has also been injured in the accident and is undergoing treatment. His condition is said to be serious. However, the Police are probing the matter.
