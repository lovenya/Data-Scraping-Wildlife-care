Aizawl: Methamphetamine tablets worth Rs 12 crore were seized in Mizoram's Champhai district and two persons were arrested, the Assam Rifles said on Saturday.ADVERTISEMENTActing on a tip-off, personnel of the Assam Rifles and Excise and Narcotics Department seized 40,400 tablets from the Zote area near the Myanmar border on Thursday, it said.Those arrested were identified as Altab Uddin (32) and Anam Uddin (32), both from Assam's Karimganj town, it added.A case was registered, and an investigation is underway, officials said.
