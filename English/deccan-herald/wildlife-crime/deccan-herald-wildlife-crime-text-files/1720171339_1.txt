Pictures of atrocities on women are coming out from all over the country from the past few days. Recently, a shocking picture has come to light from Gurugram. A young woman returning home from work was attacked by an inebriated young man at around 11:00 pm with a helmet after she refused to ride on his bike.

The accused youth also thrashed an auto driver who came to rescue the girl. This entire incident was captured on CCTV cameras.

ACP Manoj Kumar said, “Woman was severely injured and was rushed to a hospital. The victim stated that a man named Kamal, living in his neighbourhood attacked her with helmet after she refused to ride with him on his bike. FIR has been filed under various sections of IPC.”
