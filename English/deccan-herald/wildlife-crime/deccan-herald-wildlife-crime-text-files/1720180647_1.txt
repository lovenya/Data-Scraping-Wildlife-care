The Railway Protection Force (RPF) seized 6.3 kg of ganja worth Rs 3.16 lakh at the Mangaluru Junction Railway Station.ADVERTISEMENTWhen RPF team was inspecting trains along with the crime investigation bureau of Palakkad Railway Division and the Karnataka Excise Department ahead of the Independence Day, they noticed baggage in a general second class coach of train no 16516 Karwar-Yeshwantpur tri-weekly express.
