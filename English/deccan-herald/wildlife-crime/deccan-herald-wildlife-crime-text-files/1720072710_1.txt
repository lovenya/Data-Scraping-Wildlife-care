An inmate of central prison, Hindalga, attacked a warder over the alleged delay in getting him admitted to a hospital for treatment. The incident came to light on Tuesday.ADVERTISEMENTSources said Rahil alias Rohan from Hassan had been shifted to the prison recently. He was lodged in the high-security barracks. He complained of health issues on Sunday and sought permission to get admitted to hospital. As there was delay in getting permission, he attacked warder Vinod Lokapure.
