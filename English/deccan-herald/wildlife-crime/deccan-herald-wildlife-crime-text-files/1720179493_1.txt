Fear gripped locals as rowdy-sheeter Handi Anniyanna aka Annappa was brutally murdered by a six-member gang near the Vinobnagar police station in the city Thursday.ADVERTISEMENTAccording to police, the gang assaulted him with lethal weapons and hit his head severely at a police chowki hardly 100 metres from the police station.
