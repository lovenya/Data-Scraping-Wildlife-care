Chief Minister Siddaramaiah will inaugurate ‘Pusthaka Santhe’, a two-day book fair organised by Veeraloka Prathishtana, at Swabhimana Park, HSR Layout, on Saturday. ADVERTISEMENTOver 65 book stalls will be put up with publications from across the state, including Dakshina Kannada, Dharwad, Davangere and Bengaluru, a member of Veeraloka told DH. Books from varying genres such as fiction, crime thrillers, non-fiction and biographies will be on display.
