All India Majlis-e-Ittehadul Muslimeen (AIMIM) President Asaduddin Owaisi on October 04 condemned Lakhimpur Kheri violence and said that it’s a heinous crime. “I would like to go to Lakhimpur Kheri to show solidarity with the people who've been killed over there and who were really murdered by Union Minister's son. It's high time, Modi government should withdraw the three farm laws and he should remove this Minister,” he said.ADVERTISEMENT
