Tension gripped students and teachers after two wild elephants were spotted on the ‘Jnana Sahyadri’ campus of Kuvempu University in Bhadravathi taluk on Sunday night.ADVERTISEMENTAccording to the university authorities, an elephant was spotted near staff quarters around 6 pm, and a tusker was seen after a short while. A team of forest department officials rushed to the campus.
