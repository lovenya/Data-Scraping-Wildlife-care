All four have been arrested and they are being sent to jail."The woman told police that she received a call asking her to come out of her house. As she came out, two motorcycle-borne youths were there. They allegedly forcefully took her to a deserted place where she was thrashed, her clothes were torn off and she was tied to a tree, the victim told police.
