Pune: Two persons were killed when a speeding luxury car driven by a juvenile hit their motorcycle in Maharashtra’s Pune city on Sunday, police said.ADVERTISEMENTThe 17-year-old car driver was detained after a case was registered against him under relevant provisions, they said.
