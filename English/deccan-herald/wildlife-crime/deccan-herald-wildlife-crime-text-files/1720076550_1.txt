Thieves gained entry into Chamundeshwari temple at Balanjikare in Arekadu of Siddapura and stole nearly Rs 20,000 money from the offerings box. ADVERTISEMENTThe incident took place on Wednesday night. Before the act the accused covered the CCTV camera with cloth. A case is registered at Siddapura police station as per the complaint by temple committee chairman Ravikumar.
