New Delhi: A 15-year-old boy was stabbed to death by unidentified people near his house in northwest Delhi's Jahangirpuri area on Tuesday, police said.ADVERTISEMENTThe deceased, Manish Kumar, was found dead in H Block in the evening, they said.His family members took him to the hospital, where doctors declared him dead upon arrrival, the police said. A police officer said that prima facie, it is suspected to be an issue of personal enmity but the probe is being conducted from all possible angles.The police have recorded statements of all the family members of the deceased and also collected CCTV footage from the area, they said.Kumar's father Darshan Lal told reporters that they are not aware of any personal enmity he might have had with anyone.
