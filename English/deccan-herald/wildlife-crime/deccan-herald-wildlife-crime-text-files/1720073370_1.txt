Bengaluru: A 28-year-old accounts officer at a private company was among two people killed in road accidents on Saturday night.ADVERTISEMENTSarakki resident Arun Kumar was riding his two-wheeler on Anjanapura 80 Feet Road when a tipper lorry crashed into it around 11.30 pm.He fell from the two-wheeler and died afterwards. Sujit Shah, 32, a construction worker from Bihar, was travelling in his two-wheeler on Chikka Nagamangala Road when a truck struck it. He was taken to a private hospital where doctors pronounced him dead on arrival.
