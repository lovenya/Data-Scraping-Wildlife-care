The CBI team probing the Birbhum violence incident has set up a temporary camp at the government guest house in Rampurhat on March 27.ADVERTISEMENTThe team has also named 21 accused people under Sections 147, 148, 149 and other sections in an FIR on suspected offence of armed rioting.A 15-member team of the Central agency reached the crime scene in Rampurhat on March 26 to probe the case. A total of eight people were burnt to death in the Rampurhat area of West Bengal's Birbhum on March 22.
