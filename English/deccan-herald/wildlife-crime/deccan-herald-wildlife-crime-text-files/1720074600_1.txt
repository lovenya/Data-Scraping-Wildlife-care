Police have booked two men who allegedly torched documents at their former workplace after being sacked. ADVERTISEMENTThe suspects have been identified as Rahul and Peter. Said to have been disappointed about their dismissal, they entered the office when no one was present and set the valuables on fire. According to the FIR filed at the Puttenahalli police station, the suspects were sacked as they failed to meet the targets given to them. Police have registered a case and are on the lookout for the suspects.
