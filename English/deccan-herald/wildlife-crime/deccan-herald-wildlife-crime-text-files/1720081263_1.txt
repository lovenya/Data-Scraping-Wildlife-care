Forest officers, conservationists and activists will come together on June 11 and 12 to celebrate conservation and photography.ADVERTISEMENTThe two-day event, titled 'Eye for Wild' and organised at the Chitrakala Parishath, aims to spread awareness on the importance of protecting the environment and ecology."The event offers a unique opportunity to foster a collective engagement for wildlife conservation while emphasising our shared responsibility," a spokesperson for Adavi Alert Foundation, the organiser, said.The event will also see the announcement of winners of a photography competition held in April and May.
