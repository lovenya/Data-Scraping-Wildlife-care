All the victims are undertrial prisoners.Malliakarjun noted that jail officials resorted to a minor lathi charge to separate the feuding groups, as mentioned in the FIR. The injured undertrials received treatment at the prison hospital.Preliminary investigations suggest long-standing grudges as the motive behind the assault. "They belonged to two gangs, and one wanted to intimidate the other," an officer said. The Parappana Agrahara police have registered a case under sections 34 (acts done by several persons in furtherance of common intention), 323 (punishment for voluntarily causing hurt), and 324 (voluntarily causing hurt by dangerous weapons or means) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC).
