Bengaluru: Police have nabbed a two-wheeler rider who rode around masking the number plate. ADVERTISEMENTOn October 11, a social media user tagged the city police's official handle with an image of a man riding his two-wheeler with a mask covering the number plate. It was found to be within the Mahadevapura traffic police station's limits. On Wednesday, the Mahadevapura traffic police seized the bike and handed over the case to the law and order police, who filed an FIR against the vehicle owner.
