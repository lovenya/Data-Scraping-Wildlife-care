Mumbai: In a major swoop, the Directorate of Revenue Intelligence (DRI) has arrested a female passenger from Sierra Leone with cocaine to the tune of Rs 20 crore. ADVERTISEMENTThe suspect was arrested at the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport in Mumbai. Examination of her luggage revealed that the items she was carrying viz. shoes, moisturizer bottle, shampoo bottle and antiperspirants were unusually heavy and rigid.
