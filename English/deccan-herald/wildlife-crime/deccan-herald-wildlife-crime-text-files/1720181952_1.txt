Police in western Bengaluru arrested a 21-year-old man on suspicion of stealing seven two-wheelers from different parts of the city. ADVERTISEMENTIn an official statement, Byatarayanapura police said that they arrested Majeed Pasha, a resident of Goripalya, while investigating unsolved vehicle thefts.His purported confession led the police to solve five two-wheeler thefts reported from Byatarayanapura and one reported in Shivajinagar. A seventh two-wheeler theft that Pasha allegedly committed is being looked into, police said. The seven two-wheelers are worth Rs 4.3 lakh and have been seized. KR Market police had last year arrested Pasha in a dacoity case.
