The victim was rushed to a nearby hospital, where he is presently undergoing treatment," he said.Based on the complaint lodged by the victim from the ICU of a hospital at Kalyan, the police registered a case under Indian Penal Code (IPC) sections 307 (attempt to murder) and 323 (voluntarily causing hurt).The local police are carrying out a further probe into the crime.
