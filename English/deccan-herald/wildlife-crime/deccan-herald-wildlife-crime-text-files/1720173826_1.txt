The fingerprints lifted from the house of a businessman in Jayanagar helped police trace the burglar who broke in and stole 500 grams of gold.ADVERTISEMENTPolice seized 627 grams of gold jewellery from the accused John Melwin alias Kariya, a resident of Fraser Town (Pulakeshinagar) hailing from Tamil Nadu. With this, they have solved three burglary cases.Melwin singled out the house of businessman Surendra B S, which he found locked on August 6. He broke into the house between 11.30 am and 2.30 pm and stole the valuables. Police who caught Melwin on August 12, slapped a case of burglary on him and recovered the valuables.
