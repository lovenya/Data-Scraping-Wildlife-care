Bnegaluru: The Central Crime Branch (CCB) on Thursday served notices to some more people over the alleged rave party held at a farmhouse near Electronics City.ADVERTISEMENTTelugu actor Aashi Rai, who tested negative, was among the recipients of the notice, while another actor Hema Kolla received a second notice from the CCB. Hema tested positive for drugs.Earlier, the CCB issued notices to 16 people to appear for questioning in the case.Hema and 85 others who attended the party on the intervening nights of May 19 and 20 have tested positive for drug use. The CCB is probing who owns the car and how they got the MLA and a minister’s sticker.
