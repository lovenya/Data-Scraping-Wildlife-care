The Union government on Tuesday told the Lok Sabha that about 90,000 policemen as well as 15,000 public prosecutors and judicial officers will be trained to deal with cases of cyber crime.

“We have initiated a move to train 90,000 policemen to strengthen our enforcement agencies. As many as 15,000 public prosecutors and judicial officers will also be trained (cyber crime and laws),” Minister of State for Home Affairs Kiren Rijiju said.
