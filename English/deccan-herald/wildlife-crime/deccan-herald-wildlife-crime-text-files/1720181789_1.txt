Bengaluru: A four-year-old boy was run over and killed by a tractor in Anandapura in the eastern part of the city on Saturday morning. ADVERTISEMENTKR Puram police identified the victim as Dion Prajwal.The event unfolded around 9 am when a tractor bearing the registration number KA 40 T 0018, which was stationed outside Dion’s residence on 5th D Cross in Anandapura, began to reverse in an attempt to navigate to a different roadway. Unbeknownst to the driver, Dion was positioned directly behind the vehicle and was tragically run over.The victim’s father David Kumar has filed an FIR against the tractor driver Vishwanath for rash and negligent driving and causing death by negligence.
