Six people were arrested for allegedly pelting stones at policemen who had gone to the Kakrala area after some persons had blocked a road causing a traffic jam, police said on Saturday.ADVERTISEMENTAround 7 pm on Friday, a police team asked a motorcyclist to show the papers of the vehicle. Efforts are on to nab the other accused, he added.Singh said a case has been registered against 28 identified persons and 300 unidentified persons.
