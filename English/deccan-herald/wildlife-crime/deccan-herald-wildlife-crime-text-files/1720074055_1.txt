A suitcase containing counterfeit Rs 2,000 notes amounting to Rs 1 crore was found near NICE Road in Thalaghattapura, South Bengaluru, police said. ADVERTISEMENTPolice arrived at the location after a passerby informed them about the notes. They have started an investigation and are looking for the owner of the suitcase. A police officer told DH that the currency notes looked like the colour photocopies of an original Rs 2,000 note. Police have taken up a suo motu case.
