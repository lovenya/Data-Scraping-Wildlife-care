A 26-year-old engineer was mowed down by a vehicle on HMT Road in Jalahalli police station jurisdiction on Tuesday, police said.ADVERTISEMENTThe deceased, Subhash, a resident of Peenya 2nd Stage, had recently completed his engineering and had joined a factory in Peenya Industrial Town.On Tuesday, around 6.30 am, Subhash was riding on HMT Road on his electric scooter when a vehicle hit him from behind and sped away without stopping, police said.According to police, Subhash was taken to a nearby hospital, but died on the way. A case has been registered at the Jalahalli traffic police station and efforts are on to trace the vehicle.
