A body of an unidentified man, appearing to be aged around 40-45 years, was found hanging in the jungle area of the Jawaharlal Nehru University campus here on Friday, police said.ADVERTISEMENTAccording to the official, a PCR call was received at around 6.30 p.m. after which the police staff rushed to the spot."The body is yet to be identified," DCP, Southwest, Manoj C. said.Meanwhile, a Crime Team and Forensic Team of Delhi Police were also called to examine the spot."An inquest proceeding has been initiated," the DCP added.
