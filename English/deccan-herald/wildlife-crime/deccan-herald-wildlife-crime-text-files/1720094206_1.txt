The Central Crime Branch (CCB) sleuths have apprehended two persons and seized 1,500 kg ganja worth Rs 12 crore. "A drug peddler named Salman was arrested in Chamarajpet and questioned about the source of the drugs. He then revealed about the gang in Visakhapatnam. After learning about the gang from Visakhapatnam, Bengaluru cops camped for three weeks in the Andhra Pradesh city and kept track of the developments. A suspicion about an Isuzu van led to the apprehension of the accused.
