U.S. Secretary of the Interior Ken Salazar has announced a management 
plan for the National Petroleum Reserve-Alaska (NPR-A) that balances 
wildlife conservation and energy development in the biggest public 
landscape in the nation.

 

Western Arctic Alaska is one of the most important regions for wildlife 
in all the Arctic -- and for migrating birds from around the globe.  
Among the species that depend upon critical habitat within the NPR-A are
 our 'real' reindeer, or caribou as they are more commonly known in 
North America.
