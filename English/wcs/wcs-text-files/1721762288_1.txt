Dale Miquelle, Director for the WCS Russia Program and a leading researcher on Amur (Siberian) tigers, recently shared insights about his work in the Russian Far East. He's been in Russia since 1992 studying tiger populations and training young Russians to carry on the work. 



In the Q&A, Dale discusses the trends in the Siberian tiger populations over the past 70 years, threats to the big cats, and what strategies are the most successful in saving them.
