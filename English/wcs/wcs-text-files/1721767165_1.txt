The Wildlife Conservation Society Central African Republic Program is deeply saddened by the death of Mr. Éric Rama Doungous, Ecoguard team leader and head of the Base Vie location in Bamingui. He died Monday, March 20, after he and his team, on patrol in the western sector of Bamingui-Bangoran National Park, were ambushed by armed bandits.

This tragedy leaves an irreplaceable hole in Eric’s family, in the WCS CAR program, and in the pursuit of biodiversity conservation within Eric’s beloved country, CAR, and in Africa. The memory of Éric Rama Doungous will remain that of a loving husband and father, a tireless advocate for nature, and an exemplary team leader, a noble mission to which he dedicated more than 16 years of service.

On behalf of WCS CAR, we extend our sincere condolences to the family of Éric Rama Doungous, his wife and children, the conservation community in CAR, and all those who had the privilege of working with him.

Forever in our memory, Team Leader Rama.

All of WCS stands with the WCS CAR team at this difficult time.

Le programme de la Wildlife Conservation Society en République centrafricaine est profondément attristé par le décès de M. Au nom de WCS RCA, nous présentons nos sincères condoléances à la famille de RAMA DOUNGOUS Éric, à sa femme et à ses enfants, à la communauté de conversation en RCA et à tous ceux qui ont eu le privilège de travailler avec lui.
