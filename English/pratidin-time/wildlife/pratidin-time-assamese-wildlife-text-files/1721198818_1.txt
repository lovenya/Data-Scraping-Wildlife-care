This post is solely dedicated to the wildlife heroes who showed unconditional love and respect for all living beings and our planet. When disaster strikes, people aren't the only ones in danger. Floods, Earthquakes, forest fires also threaten the lives of thousands of animals–but who will come to their rescue? Yes, these wildlife heroes of Kaziranga National Park (KNP) have risked their lives to save animals both large and small–from One-Horned rhinoceros and Asian elephant to hog deer, Royal Bengal Tiger and water buffalo! They applied firsthand and courageous rescue attempts that saved the lives of thousands of innocent wild animals. Their inspiring and heartwarming tales are a must for any young animal lover.
