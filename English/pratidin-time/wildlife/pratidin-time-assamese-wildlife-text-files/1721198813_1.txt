Two Rhino Calfs, hand raised by the Centre for Wildlife and Rescue Centre (CWRC), was today trans located to Manas national park from the fringe of Kaziranga National Park, a distance of nearly 350 kilometer. Both the calf was rescued in the 2016 flood from the Bagori range of the Kaziranga National Park. One of them is a male and another female. They are now on their way and expected to reach late tonight.
