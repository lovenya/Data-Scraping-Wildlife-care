In a major development, Mahendra Kumar Yadava, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forest (PCCF) & Head of Forest Force, Assam, has been removed as the Chief Wildlife Warden (CWLW). Last year, Mahendra Kumar Yadava, an Indian Forest Service (IFS) officer of the 1989 batch, took over as PCCF and Head of Forest Force (HoFF) of the state replacing Amit Sahai, who retired on May 31, 2022.
