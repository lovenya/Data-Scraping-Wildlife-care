After Jagga Jasoos, Assam's wildlife, tea plantation, rich culture, silk industry have showcased in upcoming Hindi comedy-drama film Sui Dhaaga. To promote the upcoming film, filmmaker has made a promotional trailer video and presented the weaving village of Sualkuchi. Sui Dhaaga is based on traditional Chanderi sarees from local weavers of Chanderi, Madhya Pradesh. Through the film, director Sharat Katariya is trying to present the hard work and dedication of the local weavers.
