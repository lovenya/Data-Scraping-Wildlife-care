Mizoram, often called the "Land of Blue Mountains," offers breathtaking and exotic landscapes that make it a dynamic destination and one of India's top places to visit. The state's diverse and vibrant traditional festivals are an exceptional encounter that should not be overlooked. Mizoram is one of the seven sister states in northeast India that one must visit to explore the majestic landscapes. The Mizo people, the locals of Mizoram, are composed of several tribes that inhabit the mountains. In this article, we will be focusing on the most famous tourist attractions in Mizoram. Dampa Tiger Reserve is one of the most famous wildlife sanctuaries in Mizoram. It is home to the Royal Bengal tiger, Asian elephants, and many other rare wildlife species. The reserve is also known for its lush green forests, scenic waterfalls, and trekking trails.
