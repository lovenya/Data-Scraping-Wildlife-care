Manas National Park and Tiger Reserve in Assam’s Baksa district will remain closed every Tuesday from April 10 for all Ecotourism activities. This was informed by the Office of Field Director of the national park. On September 2022, Assam Tourism Development Corporation (ATDC) and Indian Hotel Company Limited (IHCL) had inked a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for setting-up hospitality projects at Manas National Park.
