Assam is home to several national parks and wildlife sanctuaries. It has a diverse range of flora and fauna. The wildlife sanctuaries in Assam are some of the most popular tourist destinations in the region, attracting nature enthusiasts and wildlife lovers from all over the world. These sanctuaries are not only important for their conservation efforts but also serve as a window into the natural world, allowing visitors to witness firsthand the beauty of the state's wildlife. Garampani Wildlife Sanctuary: Located in the Karbi Anglong district of Assam, Garampani Wildlife Sanctuary is home to several endangered species, including the Hoolock Gibbon and the Golden Langur.
