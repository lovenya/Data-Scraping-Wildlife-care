Adult leopard killed after being run over in Dibrugarh A speeding vehicle ran over an adult leopard at Bokpara under the Jokai Forest beat in Assam’s Dibrugarh on Saturday. A Wildlife Trust of India (WTI) veterinarian, Khanin Changmai said, “The leopard was killed after speeding vehicle run over him. His bones were broken. The animal was very badly hit by the vehicle when he was crossing the road. The carcass of the animal was burned at Jokai reserve forest as per the procedure.”
