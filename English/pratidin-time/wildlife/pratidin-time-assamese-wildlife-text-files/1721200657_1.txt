An anthrax outbreak in Pobitora Wildlife Sanctuary has led to the deaths of two feral buffaloes. The forest department has geared up to take precautionary measures to control further spread of the disease. A team of veterinary doctors has been deputed by the state government for the mass inoculation of livestock in the region. The death, which occurredlast week, due to Anthrax has been confirmed by the Forest department. Thecarcass has already been disposed as per guidelines. Forest department issued anadvisory for preventive measures to ensure that the disease doesn't spreadfurther. Veterinary department hasbeen instructed to vaccinate the domestic elephants, which has already beencomplied with. Entry of domestic animals into the reserve will also berestricted, wherever possible. The opening date of Pobitora Wildlife Sanctuary has once again postponed till further notice.
