Two youths have gone missing while taking bath in River Brahmaputra on Tuesday afternoon. The incident was reported from Chandrapur area on the outskirts of Guwahati city. The missing duo has been identified as Vibarmi Lalu and Rahul Chetry, both hailing from Shillong in Meghalaya. Unable to trace them, their friends informed authorities who arrived at the scene to take stock of the situation.
