The deputy commissioner (DC) of Morigaon district in Assam was on Wednesday directed to file a report in connection with the Pobitora Wildlife Sanctuary case where government officials created a ruckus and misbehaved with people there. Earlier on January 15, the Drivers Association of Pobitora Wildlife Sanctuary had written to Assam Chief Minister Himanta Biswa Sarma complaining about misbehavior on the part of government officials.
