The Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) arrested an absconding rhino poacher from Nagaland during an operation, reports emerged on Thursday. The CBI carried out operations against five accused involved in a rhino poaching at the request of Assam government, sources informed. During this operation, the CBI arrested an absconding rhino poacher from Lhomithi area in Dimapur. The arrested rhino poacher will be produced before the Competent Court in Dimapur in connection with his involvement in rhino poaching. A rhino was allegedly killed by some unidentified poachers and the carcass was found lying at Longkoi Tisso Gaon in Karbi Anglong. Earlier in this month, the body of a rhino poacher who escaped police custody was found floating in the Brahmaputra River in Assam.
