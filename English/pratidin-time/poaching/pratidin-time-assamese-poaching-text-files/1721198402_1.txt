The carcass of a rhino was recovered in Gai Tapu under the Kaziranga National Park of Assam, reports said on Sunday. Reportedly the rhino carcass was spotted by workers of Gai Tapu near the banks of the river. Upon receiving information, the officials of the forest department reached the spot and retrieved the carcass. According to sources, the carcass has been sent to a post-mortem report after its rhino was recovered.
