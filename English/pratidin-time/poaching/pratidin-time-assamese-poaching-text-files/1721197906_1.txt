On Monday night during an operation led by ACF Arun Bignesh and Ritupaban Bora, Dimapur police have arrested a rhino horn smuggler in Dimapur. As per reports, the accused has been identified as Bu Eingti who was involved in rhino poaching at Bagori range in Kaziranga National Park. It may be stated that, similar operation was carried out at theKaziranga National Park, and the regions of Golaghat also.
