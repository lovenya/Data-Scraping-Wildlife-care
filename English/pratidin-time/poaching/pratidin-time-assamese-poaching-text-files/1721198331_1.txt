In a tragic incident, a carcass of a rhino was recovered inside the Orang National Park on Saturday. According to preliminary investigation by the forest department the rhino is suspected to have died due to old age. The carcass of the rhino was spotted near Borkhe forest camp inside the Orang National Park.
