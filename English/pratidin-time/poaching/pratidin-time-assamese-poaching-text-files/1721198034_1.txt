In a joint operation by Jakhalabandha Police and Nagaon Police, four rhino poachers have been arrested from Jakhalabandha. The arrested have been identified as Kangburha Tokbi, Henari Ingti, Jemir Ali and Babul Ali. Kangburhaand Henari belong from Howraghat while Jemir Ali hails from Kathiatoli inKampur and Babul Ali hails from Koliabor. Policealso recovered 303 rifle from Babul Ali's house along with five rounds ofbullet. Allthe four poachers have planned for rhino poaching in Kaziranga. Policealso informed that Jemir and Babul were also held earlier for rhino poaching.
