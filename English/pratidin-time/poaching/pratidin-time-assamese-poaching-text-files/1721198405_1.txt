Chief Minister Sarbananda Sonowal on Wednesday ceremoniously distributed appointment letters to 90 new recruits of Special Rhino Protection Force (SRPF) in Guwahati. The SRPF constables will be deployed in Kaziranga National Park to protect and stop the ever increasing number of Rhino poaching in the area. Speaking on the occasion, the CM said that the newly recruited constables will be provided advanced training to tackle all sorts of situations and curb Rhino poaching in Kaziranga National Park.
