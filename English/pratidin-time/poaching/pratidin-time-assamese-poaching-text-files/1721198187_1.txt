Two smugglers involved in rhinoceros poaching were arrested in Gohpur on Saturday night. The two apprehended poachers have been identified as one Biranchi Kumbang and Kiran Pegu. Ghahigaon police arrested the two poachers. Kumbang was arrested from Gohpur's Aliguri Pichla while Pegu was nabbed from Lohitmukh's Baligaon. Both poachers were even previously held in connection to rhino poaching.
