The Hunt for Veerappan Review: "The Hunt for Veerappan" is an enthralling docu-series on Netflix, skillfully directed by Selvamani Selvaraj, that takes viewers on a gripping and humane journey into the life and legend of the infamous bandit, Veerappan. Divided into four captivating chapters, the series delves deep into the enigmatic forest king's origins, his reign of terror, and the relentless pursuit that ultimately led to his downfall. Through a combination of archival footage, expert interviews, and chilling audio recordings of Veerappan himself, the series offers a nuanced and intriguing perspective on this complex character, who continues to fascinate audiences.
