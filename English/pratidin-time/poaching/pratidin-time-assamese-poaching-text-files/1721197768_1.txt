The Kaziranga Northern Range Forest guards on Sunday arrested a rhino poacher. The poacher named Abdul Rashid alias Lathiwala was arrested from Biswanath's 1No Adabheti . Police arrested Abdul from a house at Bishwanath's  1No Adabheti  where he  was hiding. According to police , he  was involved in many poaching  activities and was involved in  killing a rhino at Sildubi.
