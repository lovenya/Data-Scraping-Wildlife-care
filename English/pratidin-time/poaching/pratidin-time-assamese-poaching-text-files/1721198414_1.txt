Congress leader Dulu Ahmed in an indirect attack to partyMLA and former Assam Forest Minister Rakibul Hussain, alleged that he was involvedin rhino poaching. Ahmed further indirectly termed Rakibul as an agent of BJP. In a series of facebook posts, AICC member Ahmed extendedhis support to MLA Rupjyoti Kurma, who recently had an exchange of words withRakibul Hussain during a party meeting. "I am with you Rupjyoti Kurmi. The rhino poacher (inour party) is actually an agent of BJP. He is destroying Congress' base in theassembly constituencies," Ahmed posted on facebook. In another post, Ahmed wrote, "The BJP will decide Congress' candidates in 2021 assembly elections through the rhino poacher of Nagaon."
