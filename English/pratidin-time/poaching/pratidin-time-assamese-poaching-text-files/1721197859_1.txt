Kaliabor Police on Monday night arrested fourwanted poachers and recovered 10 round bullets along with several documents anda four-wheeler. The arrested poachers have been identified asAnowar Hussai of Rupahi Hat, Imam Ali of Juria, Ramzan Ali of Lumding andDimapur's Dilip Boro. The arrests have been made on the basis of a complaint lodged at Jakhalabandha PS. Police informed that the arrested poachers have been involved in rhino poaching in Kaziranga National Park.
