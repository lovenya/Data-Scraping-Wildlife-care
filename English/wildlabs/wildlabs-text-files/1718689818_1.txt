The Impact of the Internet of Things Gary Atkinson, Director of Emerging Technologies at ARM, explains why we should be interested in the Internet of Things. Could it be a game changer for conservation? In his presentation at the WWF Fuller Symposium, Gary Atkinson, Director of Emerging Technologies at ARM, introduces the ‘Internet of things’ concept and how our world is becoming increasingly instrumented and connected. He discusses the potential of these instruments and the data they collect, as well as question what this means for the future in terms of privacy, access, and unintended consequences.
