UNEP, CII and WWF India launch Un-plastic Collective

Posted on 29 August 2019

The Un-plastic Collective (UPC) is a voluntary initiative launched by United Nations Environment Programme, CII and WWF India to drive corporate action toward solutions on plastic leakage.

 

With plastic pollution becoming one of the largest threats facing the planet and its biodiversity today, WWF India, UN-Environment Program-India, CII today launched the Un-Plastic Collective (UPC), at the CII Sustainability Summit. A voluntary, multi-stakeholder initiative, the platform aims to address ways to mitigate plastic pollution and its detrimental impact on nature and move towards a circular economy through corporate commitments, translating into meaningful and measurable action.
