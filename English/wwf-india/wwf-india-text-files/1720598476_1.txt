New Delhi, October 1, 2008: Air Force Bal Bharti School, Lodi Road, won Wild Wisdom – the environment quiz organized by WWF-India, TRAFFIC-India and National Zoological Park as part of the Wildlife Week Celebrations. RED Fm 93.5, a popular FM channel, was radio partner for the event. 



The quiz received tremendous response with several schools from Delhi and adjoining areas participating in the show. The award-winning teams are:



1. Air Force Bal Bharti School, Lodi Road 

2. Gyan Bharti School, Saket 

3. Springdales School



who have won a trip to Marine Camp at Beyt Dwarka in Gujarat with teachers.



The quiz was conducted by the famous quizmaster Dyu D’Cunha. 



A young participant said, “We are delighted to be a part of this quiz – my understanding of the environment and the need to conserve it has increased after my participation.” 



Mr. Samir Sinha, Head of TRAFFIC – an international organization that tracks wildlife trade gave away the prizes to the students. 



Mr. Anuj Singh, National Marketing Head and Station Head for Mumbai RED FM commenting the quiz said, “We see our association with WWF and the Wild Life week, as an extension of our brand and yet another interesting opportunity for RED FM to Bajaao for a Cause by creating awareness for the conservation of nature and wildlife amongst the people of Delhi NCR.”
