REDE Webinar Series: India and the Renewable Energy Uptake Among C&I Customers

Posted on 06 May 2020

Under the REDE initiative, WWF-India launched a series of webinars in July, to increase exchange of knowledge and discussion regarding renewable energy uptake in India, especially in the C&I segment.



The first webinar covered “Group Captive Policy for Renewable Energy”, with Managing Director, Bridge To India and Amit Maheshwari, Sr Counsel, Trilegal as the webinar panelists.







The second webinar was a follow-up to the first webinar, to address the questions raised regarding group captive policy for renewable energy.
