'Save the Tiger' with Radio Indigo



Radio
Indigo in Bangalore, India, is running an 'Engage Indigo: Get Involved,
Get Engaged' campaign, in which they support WPSI's efforts to save
India's national animal - the Tiger. Many listeners have come forward
and volunteered to make a contribution to WPSI's projects and
activities.

To take a pledge and to support the Tiger, visit www.radioindigo.fm
