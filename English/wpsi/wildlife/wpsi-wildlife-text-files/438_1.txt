Three elephants die in Alipurdur after being hit by a goods train
                        
                         

                        
                        27 Nov 2023
Rajkumar, Alipurduar: Elephant died
after being hit by a train in North Bengal. Three elephants, including
two adults, died inside the Buxa Tiger Project forest. They lost their
lives between Rajabhatkhawa and Kalchini railway stations after being
hit by a goods train going from Siliguri to Alipurduar.
