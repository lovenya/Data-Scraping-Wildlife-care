The Tribune, Chandigarh, 17-July-2011

Dehradun:
The Uttarakhand Wildlife authorities on Saturday arrested a Garhi Cantt
resident and seized leopard bones, nails and teeth from his possession.
Acting on a tip-off, the Wildlife Protection Society of India and the
Anti-Poaching Cell of the Wildlife Department in a joint operation
today arrested Rajkishore Pradhan, resident of Nai Gaun village, Garhi
Cantt, Dehradun. The leopard body parts were found from his possession.
