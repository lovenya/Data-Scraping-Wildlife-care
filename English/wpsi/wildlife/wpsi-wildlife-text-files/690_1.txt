Two Sariska tiger poachers held


The Indian Express, New Delhi, 11 October 2009

New Delhi: The CBI has arrested Jeevan and Surta, accused as the main tiger poachers at Sariska.

The
two were nabbed in Bathinda, Punjab, with help from the Wildlife
Protection Society of India. The authorities have been on the trail of
these poachers for the past five years. The CBI was given the Sariska
case after The ndian Express exposed how the reserve had lost all its
tigers to poaching.
