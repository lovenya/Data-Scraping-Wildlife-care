Jalpaiguri: 14-feet-long Royal Bengal tiger skin seized, Bhutani nationals arrested

 

16 Oct 2019


Wildlife
officials of Jalpaiguri’s Belacoba forest range seized 14-feet-long
skin tiger skin and 110 pieces of bones including the skull of a fully
matured Royal Bengal tiger. The team also apprehended two Bhutan
nationals one of which is an ex-Army man.
