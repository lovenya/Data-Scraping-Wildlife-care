Soon,
human skin will be traded, thunders Supreme Court

 
Mudit AgarwalOctober 4th, 2010
New
Delhi The Supreme Court on Monday expressed concern over declining
population of tigers and lashed out at notorious poacher Sansar Chand,
who has been convicted for trading animal’s body parts, saying a day
would come soon when human skin will be traded for commercial purposes.

“You
are selling the skin of tigers and leopards. Tomorrow, you would sell
even human skin. In this way, the population of tigers and leopards
would be wiped out in the country. There would be no tiger left in
Sariska,” a bench of Justices Markandey Katju and T S Thakur said while
dealing with the appeal of Chand.
